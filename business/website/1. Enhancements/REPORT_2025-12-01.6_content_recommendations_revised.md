## Executive Summary

This document provides revised content recommendations for TheGrantScout website. The core messaging shifts focus from specific success rate statistics to emphasizing:

- **IRS-verified data** (1.6M+ grants from Form 990-PF filings)
- **Curated matching** (5-10 high-probability opportunities, not overwhelming lists)
- **Relationship intelligence** (finding foundations already funding similar work)
- **Data depth** (85,000+ foundations, 8 years of giving history)
