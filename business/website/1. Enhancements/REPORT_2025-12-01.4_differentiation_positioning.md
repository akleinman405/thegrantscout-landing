### 4. Research-Informed Hybrid Approach (Methodology Transparency)

**What Competitors Do:**
- **Algorithm-only:** Feed data into AI, output recommendations
- **Database-only:** Search and filter, manual curation
- **Web-only:** Scrape opportunities, minimal validation

**What We Do (4-Phase Hybrid):**

```
PHASE 1: Foundation Discovery (F990 Database)
├── Query 1.6M grants for organizations like yours
├── Identify 100-200 candidate foundations
├── Filter by geography, size, mission, recency
└── Output: Foundation Target List

PHASE 2: Current Opportunity Research (Web + Direct Sources)
├── Visit each foundation's website
├── Find CURRENT opportunities (not historical)
├── Document deadlines, requirements, processes
└── Output: Active Opportunity List

PHASE 3: Relationship Intelligence (F990 Deep Dive)
├── Pull giving patterns for each foundation
├── Identify precedent grants to similar orgs
├── Calculate prior relationship signals
└── Output: Relationship Scoring

PHASE 4: Multi-Agent Validation (Quality Assurance)
├── Verify deadlines are future-dated
├── Confirm eligibility requirements
├── Validate URLs and program status
├── Cross-check against 990 data
└── Output: Validated Opportunities (70%+ pass rate)
```

**Beta Wave 1 Results:**
- Initial pass rate: 48.6% (before methodology improvements)
- Post-QA pass rate: 70%+ (with validation)
- Issues caught:
  - 30 opportunities with past deadlines (removed)
  - 35 opportunities with eligibility barriers (flagged)
  - 15 paused/discontinued programs (removed)

**Competitive Advantage:** Combines the best of database power, web research, and human validation. Competitors pick one method; we use all three.
