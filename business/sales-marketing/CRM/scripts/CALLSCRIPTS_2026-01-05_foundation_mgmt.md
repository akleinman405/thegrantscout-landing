## Objection Handlers

### "We already use Foundation Directory / Candid"
> "Great - that's the industry standard for WHO gave. We're focused on WHO SHOULD receive. Our algorithm predicts which nonprofits are likely to succeed with specific foundations based on patterns in 8.3M grants. It's predictive, not just historical."

### "Our clients do their own research"
> "Absolutely - we're not replacing that. We're the first filter. Instead of starting from 1.8 million nonprofits, we narrow it to 20-30 that match your client's specific priorities. Saves time and surfaces hidden gems."

### "We're not looking for new vendors"
> "Totally understand. I'm not asking for a contract - just wondering if you'd find it useful to see what our data shows for one of your clients' focus areas. No strings attached."

### "What does it cost?"
> "It depends on volume. For individual foundations, we're around $100/month. For partnerships that serve multiple foundations, we structure it differently. Can we start by showing you what the data looks like?"

### "Send me something"
> "Happy to. What's the best email? I'll send a sample analysis for [their specialty area] so you can see exactly what we provide."
