3. **Their clients' staff** (if adopted as internal tool)

# Pre-Call Checklist

## For Each Call:
- [ ] Research caller's recent announcements/news
- [ ] Identify 1-2 current foundation clients (likely public info)
- [ ] Prepare 2-3 questions about their grantee pain points
- [ ] Have 1 use case ready (e.g., "RPA client designing education strategy in Texas")

## Conversation Starters
1. "Your foundation clients probably struggle with nonprofit discovery—how do you help them navigate that?"
2. "I'm curious how you assess nonprofit quality for your clients. What data do you wish you had?"
3. "We found that [specific insight from our data]. Have you seen this pattern in your client base?"
4. "How do your nonprofit clients typically find foundations that might fund them?"

## Red Flags to Watch
- They already have in-house research capability (less likely to refer)
- They're building their own tools (potential competitive threat)
- Their business model relies on manual work (they might see automation as threat)

## Green Lights to Listen For
- "We need better data on our sector"
- "Our clients ask us about nonprofits all the time"
- "We'd love to offer our clients more research capabilities"
- "Foundation discovery is one of our biggest bottlenecks"
