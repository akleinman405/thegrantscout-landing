# Foundation Management Companies - Sales Research Brief
**Date:** January 2, 2026
**Purpose:** Pre-call research for 6 foundation management/community foundation organizations

---

## Executive Summary

All 6 targets share a common challenge: **foundation clients and the nonprofits they fund struggle to discover funding fits efficiently**. TheGrantScout's semantic matching algorithm (8.3M historical grants + embeddings) solves a specific pain point:

- Foundations need better ways to recommend funding opportunities to their nonprofit clients
- Nonprofits need to discover foundations beyond their traditional networks
- All 6 organizations could integrate TheGrantScout as a *value-add tool* for their clients

## Cross-Cutting Themes & Pain Points

### Universal Challenge: Funding Discovery Asymmetry
- **Foundations:** Have money but struggle to find quality grantees efficiently
- **Nonprofits:** Need funding but struggle to identify foundations beyond their networks
- **TheGrantScout plays referee:** Bridges the gap with data-driven matching

### 2026 Context (Critical for Pitch)
- **Federal Funding Crisis:** 34% of nonprofits report declining federal funding (per CEP survey)
- **Foundation Giving Up:** 87% of foundations report *increased demand* but only 53% of nonprofits feel understood
- **Diversification Imperative:** 82% of nonprofits pursuing private/corporate grants (vs. federal)
- **Burnout Reality:** 95% of nonprofit leaders concerned about staff burnout (over-researched problem = more burnout)

**Sales Angle:** *"In 2026, nonprofits need to find foundation funding faster, and foundations need smarter ways to discover grantees. TheGrantScout solves both problems at once."*

## Call Preparation: Questions to Ask

### For JP Morgan & Plante Moran (Service Providers)
1. "How do your advisors currently help foundation clients with grantee discovery?"
2. "What % of your client inquiries are about 'where should we fund?' vs. tax/compliance?"
3. "Would a white-label research tool add value to your advisory practice?"
4. "How would you position this to your clients—add-on service, included benefit, or referral?"

### For Community Foundations (Direct Users)
1. "Walk me through how a donor asks 'where should we give?' and how you currently respond."
2. "What % of your annual grantmaking is donor-driven (recommendations) vs. foundation-initiated?"
3. "How do you help your grantees find other funders?"

### Universal Question
- "If I could show you a tool that reduced research time by 80% while improving grant fit, would that be valuable?"
