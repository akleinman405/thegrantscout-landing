Added triggers for:
- Auto-populate timezone from state on insert/update
- Auto-update `last_contacted_at` and `call_count` when calls are logged

## Installing on Pixel 6a

**Use Chrome, not Firefox.** Firefox has limited PWA support on Android.

Steps:
1. Open Chrome on Pixel 6a
2. Go to your CRM URL (e.g., `https://tgs-crm.pages.dev`)
3. Tap 3-dot menu (⋮) → "Add to Home screen"
4. Tap "Add" to confirm
5. App icon appears on home screen
