## Summary

Designed a unified ICP scoring model that predicts success across all outreach channels (phone, email) and segments (nonprofits, foundation management). The model combines three components into a 0-100 score:

| Component | Points | Key Factors |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| **Base Score** | 0-40 | Sector, revenue, staff size, grant history |
| **Channel Score** | 0-30 | Phone/email accessibility signals |
| **Readiness Score** | 0-30 | Biz dev staff, grant activity, mission clarity |

---

## Key Findings from Data Analysis

### Real Call Data Insights (49 calls)

| Factor | Finding | Model Impact |
|--------|---------|--------------|
| Sector E/P | 7.2x lift for DM reach | Maximum sector score (12 pts) |
| Revenue $1M-$2M | All 3 interested orgs here | Maximum revenue score (10 pts) |
| Small staff (1-10) | 6.67x lift for interest | High staff score (8 pts) |
| Has biz dev staff | 7x more likely to show interest | Critical readiness factor (12 pts) |
| No biz dev staff | Easy reach but 0.14x interest | Low readiness score (2 pts) |
| New to foundations | 2.4x LESS likely to answer | Negative phone channel score |

### Current Model Gaps

| Issue | Current Behavior | Proposed Fix |
|-------|------------------|--------------|
| Narrow score range | 4-12 points | 0-100 points |
| No biz dev weight | Treated as positive if none | Flipped: positive if HAS staff |
| Sector neutral | All sectors equal | E/P prioritized 4x |
| No channel optimization | Single score | Separate phone/email |
| B2B placeholder | All Tier 1, Score 100 | Tiered by market size/fit |

## Model Components

### 1. Base Score (0-40 points)

Measures organizational fit.

| Sub-component | Points | Top Scorers |
|---------------|--------|-------------|
| Sector alignment | 0-12 | E, P (12 pts) |
| Revenue band | 0-10 | $1M-$2M (10 pts) |
| Staff size | 0-8 | 1-10 employees (8 pts) |
| Grant history | 0-10 | 5+ unique funders (10 pts) |

### 2. Channel Score (0-30 points)

Predicts likelihood to respond.

| Signal | Points | Rationale |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| NOT new to foundations | 5 | 2.4x more likely to answer |
| Has website | 3 | Professional/responsive |
| Has phone | 3 | Direct contact available |
| Places verified | 2 | Legitimate business |
| Small staff | 2 | DM answers phone |

### 3. Readiness Score (0-30 points)

Predicts likelihood to convert.

| Sub-component | Points | Top Scorers |
|---------------|--------|-------------|
| Has biz dev staff | 0-12 | "yes" = 12 pts |
| Recent grant activity | 0-8 | 2024 grant = 8 pts |
| Concrete mission | 0-6 | Flagged = 6 pts |
| Grant dependency | 0-4 | >75% = 4 pts |
