## Common Sale Angle Across All 6

---

## Call Prep Checklist
- [ ] Find 2nd-degree LinkedIn connections
- [ ] Search recent news (acquisitions, product launches, funding)
- [ ] Prepare 1 specific use case ("Client designing education strategy in Texas")
- [ ] Have 1 question ready ("How do your clients assess nonprofit quality?")
- [ ] Note if they mention grant research gaps = green light

---

## Green Light Phrases to Listen For
- "We wish we had better data on..."
- "Our clients ask us about nonprofits constantly"
- "Foundation discovery is time-consuming"
- "We'd love to offer more research to our clients"

## Red Flags
- "We have in-house research" (less likely to refer)
- "We're building our own tool" (potential competitor)
- No mention of nonprofit selection challenges (may not be pain point)
