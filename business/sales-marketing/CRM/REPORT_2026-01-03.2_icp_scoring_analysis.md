## Proposed New ICP Model

### Scoring Components (0-100 points)

**Base Score (0-40 points)** - Organization fit:
- Sector E/P (Education/Human Services): +12 pts
- Revenue $1M-$2M: +10 pts
- Staff 1-10 employees: +8 pts
- Established grantee (not new to foundations): +5 pts
- Has concrete mission: +5 pts

**Channel Score (0-30 points)** - Reachability:
- Has website: +5 pts
- Has phone: +10 pts
- Has email: +5 pts
- Name-based email (not info@): +5 pts
- Faith-based org (for email): +5 pts

**Readiness Score (0-30 points)** - Conversion likelihood:
- **Has biz dev staff: +12 pts** (critical signal!)
- Recent grant activity: +8 pts
- Grant dependency >75%: +5 pts
- Received capacity grant: +5 pts

| Tier | Score | Action | Expected Outcome |
|------|-------|--------|------------------|

## Key Insights

### 1. Current ICP Score is Broken
> "ICP score showed NO clear correlation with outcomes" - REPORT_2025-12-11

The current model prioritizes "underfunded" orgs, but real data shows established grantees are more responsive.

### 2. Sector is the #1 Predictor
Education (NTEE B) and Human Services (NTEE P) sectors have 7.2x lift for reaching decision makers.

### 3. "Has Biz Dev Staff" is the Conversion Key
Only 4.4% of prospects have business development staff, but they're 7x more likely to show interest when reached. This is THE signal for conversion.

### 4. Calls > Emails (But Emails Still Work)
- Calls: 6% interest rate from 49 calls
- Emails: 0.18% response rate
- However, email produced Arborbrook (beta client)
- **Recommendation:** Tier 1-3 = Call first; Tier 4 = Email only

### 5. Faith-Based Orgs Respond to Email
3 of 4 email responders were faith-based (20x lift). Consider segmenting email campaigns.
