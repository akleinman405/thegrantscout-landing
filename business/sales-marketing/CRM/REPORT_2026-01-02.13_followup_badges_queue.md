## Implementation Details

### Color Coding Logic

| Status | Class | Background | Text Color | When |
|--------|-------|------------|------------|------|
| Future | `followup-future` | Gray (#f5f5f5) | Dark gray (#737373) | Follow-up date is in the future |
| Today | `followup-today` | Orange (#fef3c7) | Dark orange (#b45309) | Follow-up date is today |
| Overdue | `followup-overdue` | Red (#fee2e2) | Dark red (#b91c1c) | Follow-up date is in the past |

### Date Formatting

- **Today:** Shows "Today"
- **Tomorrow:** Shows "Tomorrow"
- **Other dates:** Shows "Jan 5" format (short month + day)

### Badge Layout

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ 🕗 Follow-up: Tomorrow    [Reschedule]  │
└─────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

- Left side: Clock emoji + formatted date
- Right side: "Reschedule" link that opens ScheduleCallView modal
