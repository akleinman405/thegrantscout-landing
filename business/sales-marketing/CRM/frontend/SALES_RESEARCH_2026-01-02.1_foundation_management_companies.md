## Summary: Cross-Company Pain Points & Pitch Themes

### Common Pain Points Across All 6
1. **Due diligence burden** – Making decisions about which nonprofits/projects to fund is time-intensive
2. **Lack of standardized data** – Foundation funding landscape is hard to navigate
3. **Validation gap** – Nonprofits/projects claim track record but hard to verify
4. **Outreach challenges** – Nonprofits don't know foundations are open; foundations don't find quality grantees
5. **Time cost** – Foundation research is expensive (2-3 hours per foundation)

### Unified Pitch Themes by Intermediary Type

**Formation/Compliance Orgs (Foundation Group):**
- "Next step after incorporation: grant research"
- Bundle with existing services
- Target newly formed nonprofits

**DAF Sponsors (NPT, Tides):**
- "Make your donors confident their grants matter"
- Due diligence tool for grantee validation
- Risk mitigation through data

**Fiscal Sponsors (NVF, Sunflower, NYFA):**
- "Help your clients find the right foundations"
- Reduce admin burden with smart matching
- Competitive advantage in marketplace

## Recommended Call Sequence

1. **Start with easiest asks** → NYFA (niche, clear need, less political), Foundation Group (high contact ease)
2. **Move to mid-market** → New Venture Fund (strategic fit, newer leadership)
3. **Approach large players last** → NPT, Tides (institutional sales, need executive sponsorship)
4. **Skip initially** → Sunflower Services (too new, integrating, leadership transition)
