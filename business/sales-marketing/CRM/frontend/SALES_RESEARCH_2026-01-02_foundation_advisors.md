# Foundation Management & Philanthropy Advisors: Sales Research
**Prepared:** 2026-01-02
**Purpose:** Pre-call research for 6 foundation advisory firms
**Product:** TheGrantScout ($100/mo grant research reports, 8.3M grant records with semantic matching)

## Overview: The Opportunity

These 6 firms serve two key customer groups:
1. **Foundations** - they help manage grants, strategy, compliance
2. **Nonprofits** - they help with fundraising strategy and planning

**TheGrantScout value proposition for these relationships:**
- **For advisors:** Recommend to foundation CLIENTS to improve grant discovery and matching
- **For advisors:** Recommend to nonprofit CLIENTS they advise on fundraising to find new foundation sources
- **Direct opportunity:** These firms could white-label or resell TheGrantScout services

## Cross-Cutting Pain Points & Opportunities

### What These Advisors Hear From Clients

#### From Foundation Clients:
- **"We're not reaching enough qualified nonprofits"** → TheGrantScout helps them understand their competitive landscape
- **"How do we know if our grant strategy is working?"** → TheGrantScout provides benchmarks and comparative analysis
- **"We want to expand our grantmaking but don't know where"** → TheGrantScout identifies gaps and trends
- **"Compliance and grant tracking is overwhelming"** → TheGrantScout organizes grant data intelligibly

#### From Nonprofit Clients:
- **"We don't know where to find foundations"** (47% of nonprofits cite donor acquisition as biggest challenge) → TheGrantScout directly solves this
- **"We're only reaching the same old foundations"** → TheGrantScout discovers new matches
- **"We don't have data to back up our fundraising strategy"** → TheGrantScout provides evidence
- **"Our board wants to know if our asks are realistic"** → TheGrantScout shows what similar orgs received

### The Advisors' Own Pain Points
1. **Grant research is time-consuming** - They hand it off to junior staff or expect clients to do it
2. **Data is scattered** - Foundation Directory Online, Candid, GuideStar, IRS 990s - all separate sources
3. **Outdated information** - Printed directories vs. real-time data
4. **No semantic matching** - Can't automatically match nonprofit missions to foundation purposes
5. **Advising without data feels risky** - Would like to show clients evidence-based recommendations
