## Pitch Angle by Company Type

### Formation/Compliance Service (Foundation Group)
**Angle:** "Next step after incorporation"
- Bundle grant research with formation services
- Target newly formed nonprofits
- Position as "complete solution" from 501(c)(3) to first grant

### DAF Sponsors (NPT, Tides)
**Angle:** "Make donors confident their grants matter"
- Due diligence tool for grantee validation
- Donor education (show grantee legitimacy)
- Risk mitigation (background check alternative)

### Fiscal Sponsors (NVF, Sunflower)
**Angle:** "Help your clients/projects succeed faster"
- Reduce admin burden (save 10+ hours per project)
- Accelerate funding timelines
- Competitive advantage (more successful projects = better reputation)

### Niche Sponsor (NYFA)
**Angle:** "Save artists time, improve grant outcomes"
- Filter out wrong applications early
- Build confidence (data-backed decisions)
- Competitive differentiator (smart choice for artists)

## Follow-Up Sequence (If Interested)

1. **Post-Call Email** (same day)
   - Reference conversation point
   - Send sample report + case study
   - Offer specific next step (trial, demo, partnership conversation)

2. **5-Day Follow** (if no response)
   - "Checking in on the resource I sent..."
   - Ask direct yes/no: "Does this seem valuable?"
   - Offer calendar link for brief call

3. **10-Day Follow** (if still no response)
   - Brief: "Want to check if timing is bad or not a fit?"
   - Ask for referral if not interested
   - Set reminder to reach out in 60 days

## Success Indicators During Call

**Green Flags (Keep Going)**
- Asks about pricing
- Mentions specific use case/client segment
- Asks about implementation timeline
- Wants to see demo
- Asks about data sources/credibility
- References internal champion/team
- Asks about white-label options

**Yellow Flags (Proceed Cautiously)**
- "Interesting, let me think about it"
- "Need to talk to [other person]"
- "Similar tool exists but not quite right"
- "Budget constraints"
- Long pauses in conversation

**Red Flags (Close Call or Pause)**
- "We're not interested in new vendors"
- "We have competing priorities"
- "This isn't our business model"
- "Let me check with my boss" (3rd+ time)
- No response to follow-ups after 10 days
