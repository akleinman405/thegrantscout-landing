### Universal Pain Point
**Foundation selection is hard and time-consuming across the entire nonprofit ecosystem.**

Every intermediary studied manages nonprofits or donors struggling with:
- Which foundations to apply to
- How to validate nonprofit quality
- Due diligence challenges
- Time-intensive research (10-20+ hours per foundation)

**Red flags** (close call):

- "Not a fit for us"
- "Budget constraints"
