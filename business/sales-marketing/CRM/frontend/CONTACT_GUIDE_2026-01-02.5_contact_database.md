## Call Tracker Template

Use this template for each call to track progress:

```markdown
## [Company Name] - Call Log

**Date:** [YYYY-MM-DD]
**Contact Name:** [Name]
**Title:** [Title]
**Phone/Email Used:** [Method]
**Call Duration:** [Minutes]

### Call Notes
- **Opening:** [What you said]
- **Response:** [What they said]
- **Pain points discussed:** [List]
- **Objections raised:** [List + your response]
- **Interest level:** [High/Medium/Low/Not interested]
- **Next steps:** [What happens next]

### Key Quotes
- "[Quote 1]"
- "[Quote 2]"

### Follow-up Action
- [ ] Send [DELIVERABLE] by [DATE]
- [ ] Schedule meeting on [DATE]
- [ ] Revise pitch based on feedback
- [ ] Contact new person at [COMPANY]

### Call Success Indicators
- ✅ Completed call without rejection
- ✅ Got interest signal (ask, "Is this valuable?")
- ✅ Identified next step (meeting, demo, trial)
- ✅ Got contact info for other decision-maker

### Call Failure Indicators
- ❌ Call dropped/unanswered
- ❌ Clear rejection ("not interested")
- ❌ Gatekeeping ("let me ask others")
- ❌ No follow-up commitment
```

## Pre-Call Checklist

Before any call:
- [ ] Review company profile from SALES_RESEARCH document
- [ ] Know their scale/focus/customers
- [ ] Know their specific pain point
- [ ] Prepare your 30-second opener
- [ ] Have a specific ask ready
- [ ] Have follow-up collateral ready (sample report, case study)
- [ ] Test your talking points out loud
- [ ] Clear your desk of distractions
- [ ] Have company website open for reference

---

## After-Call Documentation

For every call (whether answered or not):
1. **Log in call tracker** (see template above)
2. **Update contact status** in CRM/spreadsheet
3. **Send follow-up email** within 24 hours (if interested)
4. **Set calendar reminder** for follow-up call (5-10 days)
5. **Share learnings** with team (what objections came up, what worked)
