# Sales Calling Guide: Foundation Management Companies
**Quick reference for pre-call prep and call execution**

---

## CALL OPENER (All Companies - Customize Slightly)

**Script:**
"Hi [Name], this is [Your Name] with TheGrantScout. We help nonprofits find foundations most likely to fund them using proprietary research based on 8.3 million historical grants. I'm calling because [PERSONALIZATION - see below], and I think there might be a fit for [YOUR_ORG]. Do you have 15 minutes?"

**Why it works:**
- Lead with credibility (data specificity)
- Lead with outcome (not feature)
- Acknowledge their role
- Ask for time commitment upfront

## UNIVERSAL OBJECTION HANDLING

### Objection: "We're not a grant research company"
**Response:** "Exactly. That's why this could work—it's not your core business. Your core business is [formation/grantmaking/admin]. Ours is helping nonprofits find funding. Together, we're more valuable to clients."

### Objection: "Our clients already use Foundation Directory / Guidestar"
**Response:** "Those are databases—good for searching. We're different: we analyze which foundations are *actually likely* to fund them based on semantic matching against 8.3M historical grants. It's like the difference between 'here are all foundations' and 'here are your top 20.' Clients love it."

### Objection: "This seems like a nonprofit tool, not a B2B tool"
**Response:** "Our user is the nonprofit, but our customer is you. You get more value from this partnership: your clients succeed faster, renew longer, refer more. That's B2B play."

### Objection: "Let me think about it"
**Response:** "Great. Quick question: if I got back to you with case studies showing [RELEVANT OUTCOME], would that help the conversation? [Listen for need.] Perfect. I'll send over [DELIVERABLE] and we can talk in [TIME]."

## Call Prep Checklist

Before each call:
- [ ] Research recent company news (Crunchbase, LinkedIn, website)
- [ ] Identify decision-maker and their background
- [ ] Write down 2-3 specific pain points from research doc
- [ ] Prepare 1-2 quantified outcomes (time saved, funding increased, etc.)
- [ ] Have a specific ask ready (not just "let's talk")
- [ ] Test your opener on the personalization hook—does it sound natural?
- [ ] Have competitor/alternative research (what else do they use?)
- [ ] Prepare for "I'm not the right person" redirection

---

## Success Metrics for Each Call

**Call 1-3:** Get 15-minute call without objection
**Call 4-6:** Get interest signal + next step
**Call 7+:** Get meeting with stakeholder or kill/pause with reason
