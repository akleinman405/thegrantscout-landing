### Sample Pitch Data (Ready to Use)

For any foundation, we can now generate:

```sql
-- Find nonprofits with limited funding diversity
SELECT
    r.name as nonprofit_name,
    COUNT(DISTINCT g.foundation_ein) as total_funders,
    SUM(g.amount) as total_raised,
    CASE
        WHEN COUNT(DISTINCT g.foundation_ein) = 1 THEN 'CRITICAL'
        WHEN COUNT(DISTINCT g.foundation_ein) <= 4 THEN 'HIGH'
        ELSE 'MEDIUM'
    END as vulnerability
FROM f990_2025.fact_grants g
JOIN f990_2025.dim_recipients r ON g.recipient_ein = r.ein
WHERE g.foundation_ein = '[TARGET_FOUNDATION_EIN]'
GROUP BY r.ein, r.name
HAVING COUNT(DISTINCT g.foundation_ein) <= 4
ORDER BY vulnerability, total_raised DESC;
```
