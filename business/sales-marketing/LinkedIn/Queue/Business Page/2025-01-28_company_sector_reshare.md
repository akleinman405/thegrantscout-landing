**Option B: Original post**

Grant sizes vary dramatically by sector.

We analyzed 8 million grants. Here's what we found:

Health/Medical: $158,000 average
Arts/Culture: $86,000
Environment: $70,000
Education: $51,000
Youth/Children: $43,000

The implication:

A $50K ask in healthcare? Below average.
A $50K ask in youth services? Above average.

Calibrate your expectations to your field.
