# The 2-2-1 Foundation Research Rule

**Date:** 2025-01-29
**Channel:** Company
**Format:** Text
**Pillar:** Grant Strategy

---

Before adding any foundation to your prospect list, apply the 2-2-1 rule.

**2 years of grants**
Check their last 2 years of giving.
Are they consistent? Growing? Declining?
One good year doesn't make a pattern.

**2 similar grantees**
Find at least 2 organizations like yours in their grant history.
Same sector. Similar size. Comparable geography.
No similar grantees = low fit signal.

**1 recent grant**
At least 1 grant in the most recent year available.
Foundations go dormant. Board priorities shift.
Recent activity confirms they're still giving.

If a foundation passes 2-2-1, it's worth your research time.

If not, move on.

Your time is your scarcest resource. Spend it on high-probability prospects.
