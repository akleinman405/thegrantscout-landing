# Foundation Spotlight: Texas

**Date:** 2025-01-14
**Channel:** Company
**Format:** Text
**Pillar:** Foundation Intelligence

---

Texas has 12,170 private foundations.

That's third in the nation - behind only California and New York.

Here's what we found in the data:

→ Texas foundations gave $22.7B in grants (2016-2023)
→ Average grant size: $44,000
→ 55% of Texas foundation grants stay in-state
→ Top sectors: Education, Health, Religion

The opportunity:

Texas is a foundation powerhouse that's often overlooked.

Many have no website (harder to find via Google).
Many prefer local organizations.
Many have simpler application processes.

If you're a Texas nonprofit, you have 12,000+ local options.

If you're not in Texas, here's the bigger insight:

45% of Texas grants cross state lines.

That's nearly half of $22.7 billion going to organizations outside Texas.

Don't overlook the Lone Star State just because you're not there.
