# Data Insight: The Hidden Foundation Problem

**Date:** 2025-01-21
**Channel:** Company
**Format:** Text
**Pillar:** Foundation Intelligence

---

Here's a problem most grantseekers don't know they have:

43% of foundations have no website.

No website = invisible to Google.
No website = no online application.
No website = you have to dig to find them.

But these foundations still give grants.

They're often:
→ Family foundations with simpler processes
→ Smaller but less competitive
→ Relationship-driven (LOI over formal apps)

How to find them:

1. 990-PF database research
2. State attorney general nonprofit registries
3. Community foundation referrals
4. Professional network recommendations

The foundations hiding in plain sight might be your best prospects.
