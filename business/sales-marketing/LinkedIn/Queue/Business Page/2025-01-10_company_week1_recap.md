# Week 1 Recap: What We Learned

**Date:** 2025-01-10
**Channel:** Company
**Format:** Text
**Pillar:** Foundation Intelligence

---

Our first week sharing foundation data insights.

Here's what stood out:

The median foundation grant is $3,500.
(Not $58K - that's the average, skewed by mega-grants)

53% of all grants are under $5,000.

82% are under $25,000.

The takeaway:

Most foundations give smaller amounts than you think.

That's not bad news - it's strategic intelligence.

Smaller asks = more foundations in your pipeline.
