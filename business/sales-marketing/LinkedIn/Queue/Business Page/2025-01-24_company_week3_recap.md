This week we explored two big questions:

**Where is the money concentrated?**
→ Top 1% of foundations = 67% of all dollars
→ But the "middle tier" (2-25%) is less competitive
→ Strategic opportunity: target foundations others overlook

**Why are some foundations invisible?**
→ 43% have no website
→ You can't find them on Google
→ But they're still giving—often with simpler processes

**The relationship insight:**
→ 50% of grants are one-time
→ Second grant = gateway to long-term relationship
→ Stewardship matters as much as prospecting
