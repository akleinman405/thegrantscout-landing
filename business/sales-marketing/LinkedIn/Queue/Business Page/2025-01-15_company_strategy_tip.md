# Strategy Tip: The 3-Source Validation Rule

**Date:** 2025-01-15
**Channel:** Company
**Format:** Text
**Pillar:** Grant Strategy

---

Before adding a foundation to your prospect list, validate with 3 sources.

Here's the framework:

**Source 1: 990-PF Data**
→ Verify they're actively giving
→ Check grant sizes match your ask
→ Confirm geographic alignment

**Source 2: Website/Guidelines**
→ Read current priorities
→ Note application process
→ Check deadline timing

**Source 3: Grant History**
→ Who have they funded recently?
→ What's the typical grant size?
→ Do they repeat-fund grantees?

Why this matters:

990 data can be 1-2 years old.
Websites can be outdated.
Neither tells the full story alone.

Cross-reference all three before investing time in an application.
