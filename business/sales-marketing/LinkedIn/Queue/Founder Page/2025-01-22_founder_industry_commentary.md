# The Grant Writing Industry Has a Discovery Problem

**Date:** 2025-01-22
**Channel:** Founder
**Format:** Text
**Pillar:** Sector Trends

---

I've been thinking about why foundation prospecting is so hard.

It's not a research problem. The data exists.
It's not an access problem. 990s are public.

It's a discovery problem.

143,000 foundations.
8 million grants.
All documented in IRS filings.

But to find the right 20 for your nonprofit?

You need to:
→ Know what to search for
→ Understand giving patterns
→ Match priorities to your work
→ Track who's actually giving

Most tools give you a list.
Lists don't tell you fit.

That's why we're building intelligence, not just a database.

The data is there. The insight is what's missing.
