# Origin Story - Why I'm Building TheGrantScout

**Date:** 2025-01-06
**Channel:** Founder
**Format:** Text
**Pillar:** Founder Journey

---

I come from a data analytics background.

I've seen what happens when you apply real-world data to decision-making.
Better predictions. Measurable outcomes. Less guessing.

Then I looked at the nonprofit space.

There's an incredible amount of data available.
Every private foundation files an IRS 990-PF.
Every grant they've ever made is public record.
8 million grants. 143,000 foundations. 8 years of giving history.

But almost nobody is using it.

Nonprofits are still cold-applying to foundations based on Google searches and word of mouth.
They have no idea which foundations have funded organizations like theirs.
No idea which ones prefer their geography, their mission, their budget size.

The data exists. It's just buried.

That's why I'm building TheGrantScout.

We analyze foundation giving patterns to help nonprofits find funders they didn't know existed.

I'll be sharing what we learn from the data here.

Follow along if you're curious about what the numbers reveal.
