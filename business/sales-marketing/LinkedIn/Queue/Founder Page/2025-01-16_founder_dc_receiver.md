# DC Receives More Foundation Money Than Any Other Place

**Date:** 2025-01-16
**Channel:** Founder
**Format:** Text
**Pillar:** Foundation Intelligence
**Insight #:** 13 (revised)

---

Washington, DC receives $17.7 billion more in foundation grants than its foundations give out.

That's the largest net positive in the country.

Why? National nonprofits.

Foundations in New York, California, and Washington State fund organizations in DC because that's where the national nonprofits are headquartered.

DC isn't alone. Other net receivers include:
→ Massachusetts: +$10.2B
→ Virginia: +$3.5B
→ Georgia: +$2.3B
→ Ohio: +$2.1B

Meanwhile, the biggest net exporters:
→ New York
→ Washington State (Gates effect)
→ California

These three states send hundreds of billions to organizations nationwide.

What this means for you:

If you're in a "receiver" state like DC or Massachusetts, out-of-state foundations should be at the top of your prospect list.

If you're anywhere else, the data is clear: foundations give everywhere.

48% of all grants cross state lines.

Your next funder might be 2,000 miles away.
