# What I Learned From Our First Beta Tester

**Date:** 2025-01-17
**Channel:** Founder
**Format:** Text
**Pillar:** Founder Journey

---

Our first beta tester taught me something I didn't expect.

She's a grant writer with 15+ years of experience.
She knows her funding landscape cold.

I asked: "What would make this useful for you?"

Her answer surprised me:

"I don't need you to find me new foundations. I know most of them."

"I need you to help me not miss anything."

She wanted organization, not discovery.

A calendar. Reminders. Nothing falling through the cracks.

That's when I realized:

We're not just building a prospecting tool.

We're building a grant intelligence system.

Discovery for those who need it.
Organization for those who don't.
