# General Support Is 20% of All Foundation Giving

**Date:** 2025-01-28
**Channel:** Founder
**Format:** Text
**Pillar:** Grant Strategy
**Insight #:** 10

---

1.6 million grants totaling $57.6 billion.

All labeled "general operating support."

That's 20% of all foundation giving.

The average general support grant? $35,000.

Why this matters:

Foundations DO give unrestricted support.
A lot of it.

If you're only applying for program-specific grants, you're leaving money on the table.

General support asks:
→ Simpler to apply for
→ More flexible for you
→ More common than you think

Look for foundations with "general operating" in their grant histories.
