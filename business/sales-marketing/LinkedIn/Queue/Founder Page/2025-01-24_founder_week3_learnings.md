# Week 3: What the Data Keeps Teaching Me

**Date:** 2025-01-24
**Channel:** Founder
**Format:** Text
**Pillar:** Founder Journey

---

Three weeks of sharing foundation data.

Here's what keeps surprising me:

1. **Concentration is extreme**
The top 1% of foundations give 67% of dollars.
Everyone competes for the same few.

2. **Visibility is optional**
43% of foundations have no website.
They're giving grants. Just quietly.

3. **Relationships beat applications**
50% of grants are one-time.
But get a second? You're likely to get 5+.

The pattern I see:

Most nonprofits chase visible, competitive opportunities.

The smart ones find hidden, relationship-driven ones.

That's the gap we're trying to close.
