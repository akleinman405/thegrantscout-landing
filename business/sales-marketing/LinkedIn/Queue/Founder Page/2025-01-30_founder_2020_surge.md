# The 2020 Surge: Foundation Giving Nearly Doubled

**Date:** 2025-01-30
**Channel:** Founder
**Format:** Text
**Pillar:** Sector Trends
**Insight #:** 17

---

Foundation giving nearly doubled in 2020.

2019: $54.1 billion
2020: $94.6 billion

The COVID response was real.

But here's what happened after:

2021: $36.9 billion (dip)
2022: $117.8 billion (new high)
2023: $119.0 billion (continued growth)

What the data shows:

Foundation giving has recovered.
And exceeded pre-pandemic levels.

Average grant size in 2018: $41K
Average grant size in 2023: $74K

Grants are getting larger.

The question: Is this consolidation (fewer, bigger grants) or inflation adjustment?

Either way, don't anchor to old benchmarks.

The landscape has shifted.
