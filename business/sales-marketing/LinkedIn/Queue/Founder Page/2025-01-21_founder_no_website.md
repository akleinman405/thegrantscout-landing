# 43% of Foundations Have No Website

**Date:** 2025-01-21
**Channel:** Founder
**Format:** Text
**Pillar:** Foundation Intelligence
**Insight #:** 20

---

43% of private foundations have no website.

That's 13,000+ foundations you can't find on Google.

We analyzed 30,000+ foundation filings.

57.4% have a website listed.
42.6% have no web presence at all.

This is why grant databases exist.
This is why 990-PF research matters.

If you're only using Google to find foundations, you're missing almost half of them.

The good news?
99.5% of grants have purpose descriptions in their IRS filings.

The information exists.
It's just buried.
