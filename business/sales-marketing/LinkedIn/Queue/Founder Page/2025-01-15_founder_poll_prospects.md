**Follow-up post (schedule for Jan 17 or after poll closes):**

The responses are in.

Here's what's interesting:

Each method has tradeoffs.

Google: Fast, but misses 43% of foundations (no websites).

Grant databases: Comprehensive, but expensive and crowded.

Peer recommendations: High quality, but limited scale.

990-PF research: Most complete, but time-intensive.

The best prospectors use all four.

But they start with 990-PF data, then validate with the others.
