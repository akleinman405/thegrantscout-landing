# Month 1: What I've Learned Sharing Foundation Data

**Date:** 2025-01-31
**Channel:** Founder
**Format:** Text
**Pillar:** Founder Journey

---

One month of sharing what I've learned from 8 million grants.

Here's what surprised me most:

**The data resonates**
The median vs. average post got more saves than anything else.
People want to calibrate expectations to reality.

**Geography is undervalued**
The cross-state and Texas posts sparked real conversations.
Most people underestimate how far foundation dollars travel.

**Relationships matter more than I thought**
The 50% one-time stat hit a nerve.
Everyone knows stewardship matters. Few have data to prove it.

**What I'm still figuring out:**

How to turn insights into action.
Data is interesting. Strategy is useful.

I'm working on making the connection clearer.

Thank you for following along.
