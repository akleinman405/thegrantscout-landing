**Channel:** Founder

Which state has the most private foundations?

California. With 14,180.

The top 5:
1. California: 14,180
2. New York: 13,788
3. Texas: 12,170
4. Florida: 10,593
5. Pennsylvania: 8,271

That's 60,000+ foundations in just 5 states.

Why this matters:

No matter where you are, you have options.

The coastal states lead in foundation counts.
But Texas and Florida are close behind - and often overlooked.

And here's the bigger insight:

48% of foundation grants cross state lines.

A foundation in California might fund your work in Ohio.
A foundation in New York might support your mission in Georgia.

Don't limit your search to local funders.

The data shows: geographic boundaries matter less than you assume.
