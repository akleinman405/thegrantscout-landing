# The Average Grant is $58K. The Median? $3,500.

**Date:** 2025-01-07
**Channel:** Founder
**Format:** Text
**Pillar:** Foundation Intelligence
**Insight #:** 6

---

The average foundation grant is $58,000.

The median? Just $3,500.

We analyzed 8.3 million grants to find this.

Why the gap matters:

A few mega-grants ($1M+) skew the average.
They represent only 0.7% of all grants.
But they account for 61% of all dollars.

Most foundations give smaller amounts:
→ 53% of grants are under $5,000
→ 82% are under $25,000

What this means for your strategy:

A $5,000 ask isn't lowballing.
It's realistic for most foundations.

Don't anchor to the "average."
Anchor to reality.
