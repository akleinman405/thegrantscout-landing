# 50% of Foundation Relationships Are One-Time Grants

**Date:** 2025-01-23
**Channel:** Founder
**Format:** Text
**Pillar:** Foundation Intelligence
**Insight #:** 8

---

50% of foundation-grantee relationships are one-time grants.

We analyzed 1.95 million foundation-recipient pairs.

Single grant only: 49.5%
2 grants: 19.2%
3-5 grants: 24.5%
6-10 grants: 6.1%
10+ grants: 0.8%

Here's what this tells us:

Getting the FIRST grant is hard.

But if you get a second one?
You're likely to get many more.

31% of relationships involve 3+ grants.

Focus on:
→ Building relationships, not just applications
→ Stewardship after the first grant
→ Becoming a repeat grantee, not a one-time ask

The second grant matters more than the first.
