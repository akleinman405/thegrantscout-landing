# Why Timing Matters More Than Your Proposal

**Date:** 2025-01-10
**Channel:** Founder
**Format:** Text
**Pillar:** Grant Strategy

---

Most grant deadlines are spring and fall.

Most grantseekers reach out 2 weeks before.

Smart grantseekers reach out 6 months before.

Here's why timing matters:

Foundation program officers review hundreds of proposals.

By deadline day, they're overwhelmed.

6 months out? They have time to:
→ Answer your questions
→ Suggest positioning
→ Remember your name

The proposal matters.
But getting on their radar early matters more.
