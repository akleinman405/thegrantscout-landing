**Date:** 2025-01-08

**Pillar:** Foundation Intelligence
**Insight #:** 6

---

**Question:**
What's the median private foundation grant in the US?

**Options:**
○ $50,000
○ $25,000
○ $10,000
○ $3,500

---

**Follow-up post (schedule for Jan 10 or after poll closes):**

The answer surprised most people.

It's $3,500.

Not $50K. Not $25K.

The average is $58,000 - but that's skewed by mega-grants.

53% of all foundation grants are under $5,000.

What this means: A $5K ask is normal. A $10K ask is above average.

Calibrate your expectations to reality, not headlines.
