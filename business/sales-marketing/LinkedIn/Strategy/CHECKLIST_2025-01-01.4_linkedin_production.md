## Text Post Checklist

### Before Writing
- [ ] Identify insight/topic from catalog
- [ ] Determine hook (first line)
- [ ] Decide on post length (800-1200 chars optimal)

### Writing
- [ ] Start with hook (most interesting finding first)
- [ ] Use short sentences (under 15 words)
- [ ] Add line breaks every 1-2 sentences
- [ ] Include specific numbers
- [ ] End with takeaway or question
- [ ] Keep under 1200 characters

### Formatting
- [ ] No walls of text
- [ ] Use → or • for lists (sparingly)
- [ ] No emojis (unless specifically testing)
- [ ] No excessive punctuation!!!

### Final Check
- [ ] Hook is compelling in feed preview (first ~150 chars)
- [ ] Read aloud - does it sound conversational?
- [ ] Clear takeaway for reader
- [ ] Add 3-5 hashtags at end

## Poll Checklist

### Planning
- [ ] Identify topic from poll catalog
- [ ] Write question (under 100 chars)
- [ ] Create 4 options (mutually exclusive)
- [ ] Know which answer is "correct" (for follow-up)

### Structure
```
Question: [Clear, specific question]

○ Option 1
○ Option 2
○ Option 3
○ Option 4
```

### Best Practices
- [ ] Question provokes curiosity
- [ ] Options are distinct and clear
- [ ] One option is surprisingly correct
- [ ] Plan follow-up "reveal" post

### Follow-Up
- [ ] Schedule reveal post for 2-3 days later
- [ ] Reference poll results in reveal
- [ ] Provide data behind correct answer

## Daily Engagement Routine (30 min)

### Morning (15 min)
- [ ] Check notifications
- [ ] Respond to all comments on your posts
- [ ] Reply with 15+ words (substantive)

### Throughout Day (15 min)
- [ ] Like 10-15 relevant posts
- [ ] Comment on 3-5 posts in your niche
- [ ] Comments should be 15+ words, add value
- [ ] Check DMs

### Good Comment Examples
```
"This resonates. We've seen similar patterns in our foundation data -
the foundations with clearer giving priorities tend to have higher
repeat funding rates. Have you noticed this correlation?"
```

### Bad Comments (Avoid)
- "Great post!"
- "Thanks for sharing"
- "So true!"
- "Love this"
