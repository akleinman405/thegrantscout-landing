## Notes

### Strengths of This Plan
1. **Launch week fully scripted** - Can execute immediately
2. **Database insights integrated** - Unique content no competitor has
3. **Dual-channel coordination** - Clear roles for founder vs company
4. **Realistic time commitment** - ~6.5 hours/week is sustainable

### Risk Factors
1. **Carousel production bottleneck** - Consider batching on weekends
2. **Engagement time** - Critical but easy to skip; schedule it
3. **Content bank depletion** - Maintain 1-week buffer always

### Recommendations for Execution
1. **Batch create carousels** - Do 2-4 at once on weekends
2. **Use scheduling** - Post at optimal times even when busy
3. **Track what works** - Double down on high performers
4. **Repurpose winners** - Turn great posts into carousels and vice versa
