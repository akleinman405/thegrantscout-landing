## Top 5 Surprising Findings

### 1. Median Grant is Only $3,500
- Average: $58,416
- Median: $3,500
- **Insight:** Most grants are small; average is skewed by mega-grants

### 2. Texas Has More Foundations Than California
- Texas: 3,780 foundations
- California: 2,827 foundations
- **Insight:** Challenges assumption about foundation geography

### 3. Top 1% Give 67% of All Dollars
- 1,125 foundations give $325B (67.1% of total)
- Bottom 75% (84,310 foundations) give only $20B (4.2%)
- **Insight:** Extreme concentration at the top

### 4. 48% of Grants Cross State Lines
- In-state: 52.4%
- Out-of-state: 47.6%
- **Insight:** National prospecting is worthwhile, not just local

### 5. DC is the Only Net Receiver
- DC receives $3.75B MORE than it gives
- Every other state is a net exporter of foundation dollars
- **Insight:** Geographic advantage for DC-based nonprofits
