### Phase 1: Competitor Research
**Objective:** Understand what works in grant-tech/nonprofit LinkedIn space

**Key Findings:**
- Instrumentl leads with 4,337 followers, 5:1 educational-to-promotional content ratio
- Bloomerang proves nonprofit SaaS can reach 13,140 followers with daily posting
- Personal profiles get 5x more engagement than company pages
- Optimal: 3-5x/week, Tuesday-Thursday 9AM-12PM, carousels outperform text
