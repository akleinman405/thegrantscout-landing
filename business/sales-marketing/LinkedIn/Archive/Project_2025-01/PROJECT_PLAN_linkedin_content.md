# LinkedIn Content Strategy Project Plan
## TheGrantScout - Q1 2025

### Project Goal
Develop a 3-month LinkedIn posting plan with researched content strategy, database-driven insights, and production schedule.

---

## Phase 1: Research & Discovery
**Objective:** Understand what works in the grant-tech/nonprofit space on LinkedIn

**Tasks:**
1. Analyze competitor LinkedIn presence (from COMPETITOR_DATABASE.csv):
   - Instrumentl, Candid, GrantWatch, OpenGrants, Granter.ai, GrantStation
   - Document: posting frequency, content types, engagement patterns, tone
2. Research LinkedIn B2B best practices (3-5 authoritative sources)
3. Identify patterns specific to nonprofit/foundation sector

**Deliverable:** `REPORT_2025-01-01_linkedin_competitor_research.md`

**Checkpoint:** Review report, approve findings before Phase 2

---

## Phase 2: Content Strategy Framework
**Objective:** Define content pillars and post types for TheGrantScout

**Tasks:**
1. Define 4-6 content pillars (e.g., data insights, industry tips, founder journey, customer wins)
2. Map content types to pillars (carousel, text-only, polls, video, infographics)
3. Identify content sources:
   - Database queries (f990_2025 data insights)
   - External research
   - Original thought leadership
   - Customer stories
4. Define brand voice and tone guidelines

**Deliverable:** `SPEC_2025-01-01_linkedin_content_framework.md`

**Checkpoint:** Review framework, approve strategy before Phase 3

---

## Phase 3: Database-Driven Content Mining
**Objective:** Extract compelling data insights from f990_2025 for posts

**Tasks:**
1. Brainstorm 20-30 data questions that make interesting posts
2. Write and execute SQL queries to answer each
3. Identify surprising/counterintuitive findings
4. Format insights into post-ready statistics

**Deliverable:** `DATA_2025-01-01_linkedin_insights_catalog.md`

**Checkpoint:** Review insights, select best for calendar before Phase 4

---

## Phase 4: Calendar & Execution Plan
**Objective:** Build actionable 3-month posting schedule

**Tasks:**
1. Set posting cadence (e.g., 3x/week)
2. Map content pillars across calendar for variety
3. Assign specific post topics to dates
4. Create production checklist per post (copy, graphics, data, approvals)
5. Identify dependencies and lead times

**Deliverables:**
- `CALENDAR_2025-01-01_linkedin_q1.md` (full schedule)
- `CHECKLIST_2025-01-01_linkedin_production.md` (execution guide)

**Final Checkpoint:** Review complete plan, approve for execution

---

## Success Metrics (Post-Execution)
- Follower growth rate
- Engagement rate per post type
- Website clicks / demo requests attributed to LinkedIn
- Content production efficiency
