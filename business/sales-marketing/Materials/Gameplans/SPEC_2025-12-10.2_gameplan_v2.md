## 1. Network Prospects (Priority 1)

**Goal:** Immediate revenue from warm connections

### Build the List
- [ ] LinkedIn connections audit (filter for nonprofit titles)
- [ ] Friends/family working at or connected to nonprofits
- [ ] Beta clients' connections (ask for intros)
- [ ] Beta clients' funders → their other grantees (F990 query)

### Outreach
- Warm text/call: "Hey, I'm helping nonprofits find grant opportunities — know anyone who'd benefit?"
- LinkedIn DM to connections at nonprofits
- Email beta clients asking for 1 referral each

### What You Need
- [ ] Nothing — just start reaching out

---

## 2. Foundation Email Campaign (Priority 2)

**Goal:** $12K+ deals in 3-6 months

### Build the List
- [ ] Use Research Team output (50 foundations with contacts)
- [ ] Prioritize Tier 1: Baton Rouge, Omaha, New Haven, Atlanta, Ozarks
- [ ] Gather: Name, program officer, email, program name, timing

### Set Up Campaign
- [ ] Write email template (personalized per foundation)
- [ ] Create foundation one-pager (attach to emails)
- [ ] Load into email campaign script

### Email Template
```
Subject: Grant discovery for [Program Name] grantees

Hi [Name],

I saw [Foundation] runs [Program Name] — [specific detail about their program].

I run TheGrantScout, a grant discovery service that helps nonprofits find foundation funding they don't know about. We analyze IRS 990 data to match nonprofits with foundations funding similar work.

Would this be valuable for your grantees? Happy to do a free webinar or pilot with a few orgs.

15 minutes to explore?

Best,
Alec
```

### What You Need
- [ ] Foundation one-pager (1-2 hours to create)
- [ ] 2-3 beta testimonial quotes
- [ ] Email campaign script configured

## 3. Nonprofit Email Campaign (Priority 3)

**Goal:** Steady $1.2K clients, compare ROI to foundations

### Build the List
- [ ] F990 query: Revenue $500K-$5M, few foundation grants, priority states
- [ ] Export 1,000-2,000 targets
- [ ] Enrich with emails via email finding script

### Set Up Campaign
- [ ] Write email template (personalized by sector/state)
- [ ] Load into email campaign script
- [ ] Set up 3-email sequence

### Email Template
```
Subject: Found [X] foundations funding [sector] nonprofits in [state]

Hi [Name],

I help nonprofits find foundation funding they don't know about.

I ran a quick search and found [X] foundations in [state] that fund [sector] work like [Org Name]'s. Most nonprofits I talk to know maybe 20% of the foundations that could fund them.

Want me to send you the list? Takes 15 minutes to walk through.

Best,
Alec
```

### What You Need
- [ ] F990 query run (Research Team or Dev Team)
- [ ] Email finding script ready ✓
- [ ] Email campaign script configured
