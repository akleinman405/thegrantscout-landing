# TheGrantScout
## Grant Discovery for Your Grantees

---

### The Problem

Your grantees struggle to find new funding sources. Most nonprofits know only a fraction of the foundations that could fund their work — they apply to the same funders year after year, missing opportunities that match their mission.

Limited development staff means limited time for prospect research. Your capacity-building investment could go further if grantees had better funding pipelines.

---

### The Solution

TheGrantScout helps nonprofits discover foundation funding they don't know about.

We analyze **1.6 million grants** from IRS 990-PF filings to identify foundations that:
- Fund similar organizations
- Give in their geographic area
- Support their program areas
- Have a history of funding new grantees

Each nonprofit receives a **monthly report** with 5-10 matched opportunities, funder intelligence, and positioning recommendations.

---

### What Your Grantees Get

| Deliverable | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Matched Opportunities** | Foundations actively funding work like theirs |
| **Funder Profiles** | Giving history, grant ranges, geographic focus |
| **Positioning Strategy** | How to approach each funder based on their patterns |

Our database includes:

We help nonprofits find funders who are already supporting similar work — not cold prospects, but warm matches.
