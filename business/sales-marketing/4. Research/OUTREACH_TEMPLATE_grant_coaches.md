## FOLLOW-UP SEQUENCE

### If No Response After 1 Week:
Send 1 follow-up email or LinkedIn message with:
- Link to brief demo or case study
- Specific example of how it would help their client type

### If No Response After 2 Weeks:
- Move to lower priority list
- Try alternative contact method (LinkedIn if emailed, email if LinkedIn)
- Consider outreach in 60 days

### If Response is "Interested":
- Schedule brief call (15-20 min)
- Share: demo access, affiliate commission structure, case studies
- Send: one-page partnership overview
- Follow up within 48 hours of call

### If Response is "No Thanks":
- Gracious thanks and keep on list for 6-month re-outreach
- Note reason for decline (price, service overlap, business model)

## NEXT STEPS AFTER CONTACT

1. **If interested in partnership:** Develop formal agreement with:
   - Commission structure (referral % or per-signup)
   - Co-marketing approach
   - Exclusive vs. non-exclusive territory
   - Term length

2. **If interested in integration:** Plan technical/operational integration:
   - API access?
   - Embedded tool in their platform?
   - Affiliate link only?
   - Co-branded offering?

3. **If interested in demo:** Provide:
   - Guided product tour (20-30 min)
   - Case study of nonprofit/coach using TheGrantScout
   - Pricing and partnership options
   - Trial access for their community
