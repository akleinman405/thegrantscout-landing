## KEY INSIGHTS FROM RESEARCH

1. **Market Opportunity:** Grant writing consultant market is booming - 145,000+ TGCI alumni, 3,400+ trained through Learn Grant Writing, multiple 20-50 year old coaching practices.

2. **Complementary Services:** Nearly all grant writing coaches/consultants focus on the WRITING side. Prospect research/foundation finding is an adjacent but separate need. TheGrantScout solves their upstream problem.

3. **Online Presence Requirement:** Coaches with strong online presence (blogs, newsletters, social media, courses) are best positioned to refer clients. Queen Israel, Dr. Browning, Margit Brazda, and Learn Grant Writing all have active content channels.

4. **Community Models:** Several coaches (Holly Rustick, Learn Grant Writing, Rodney Walker) operate community/membership models with multiple clients - excellent for referral partnerships.

5. **Pricing Sweet Spot:** At $99/month, TheGrantScout is premium enough to not cannibalizes coaches' services but affordable for nonprofits working with coaches. Bundle potential: "$199/month for grant writing coaching + research" could work.

6. **Specialization Angles:**
   - BIPOC-focused nonprofits (Sheleia Phillips/SMP)
   - Federal grants (Holly Rustick, Dr. Rice)
   - Nonprofit capacity building (Capacity+, Dr. Young/Myrick)
   - Freelance consultant training (Learn Grant Writing, Holly Rustick)
