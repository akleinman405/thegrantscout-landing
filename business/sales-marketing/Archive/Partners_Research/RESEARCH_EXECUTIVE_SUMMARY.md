### Competitive Intelligence
**Direct Competitors:**
- **Instrumentl:** $55M funded, 4,500 customers, $1B+ in grants won by users, comprehensive platform (discovery + writing + tracking)
- **Candid:** Institutional data provider, well-established, not AI-powered

**Differentiation Opportunity:**
TheGrantScout fills the "initial matching" gap with **AI-powered automation for mid-market nonprofits** that can't afford:
- Expensive consulting ($5K-10K per project)
- Instrumentl's premium pricing ($500-2000+/month)
- Manual 20-hour research cycles

**1. Creator Tier Analysis**
- **Tier 1 (5 creators):** 80K-Popular subscriber bases, direct audience alignment, proven influence

## Risk Mitigation

**Risk:** Creators say "no"
**Mitigation:**
- Have 3x more outreach targets than conversion goal
- Frame as low-lift (guest appearance = 1 hour)
- Offer high-touch support for partners

**Risk:** Competitor positioning (Instrumentl)
**Mitigation:**
- Lead with differentiation (not competition)
- Position as "feeder" not "replacement"
- Explore integration rather than competition

**Risk:** Low response rate
**Mitigation:**
- Highly personalized outreach (not template)
- Multiple touch points (email, LinkedIn, Twitter)
- Warm introductions where possible

**Risk:** Integration/tech challenges
**Mitigation:**
- Vet partner tech stacks before committing
- Build APIs/integrations early
- Have fallback positioning (affiliate only)
