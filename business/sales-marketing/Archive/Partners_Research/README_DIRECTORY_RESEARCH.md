## Positioning Summary

**TheGrantScout Unique Position:**
- **Data Advantage:** Real IRS 990-PF data (143,000 foundations, 8.3M historical grants)
- **Specialization:** Foundation research only (vs. multi-source competitors)
- **AI Matching:** Semantic matching on nonprofit mission + foundation preferences
- **Affordability:** $99/month (undercuts Instrumentl at $179)
- **Market Position:** "Affordable foundation research for every nonprofit budget"

**vs. Competitors:**
- Instrumentl = Multi-source (federal, state, corporate, foundation) at $179/month
- GrantStation = Foundation + corporate research at $150+/month
- Foundation Directory Online = Research depth at $55+/month
- TheGrantScout = Specialized foundation focus + AI matching at $99/month
