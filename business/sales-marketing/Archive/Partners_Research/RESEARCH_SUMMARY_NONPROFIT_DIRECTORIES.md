## Key Insights

### Market Gaps Identified
1. **Affordability Gap:** No major platform focuses on sub-$100/month grant research solutions
2. **Specialization Gap:** Most tools are multi-source (federal, state, corporate, foundation); none specialize in 990-PF/foundation-only research
3. **AI Positioning Gap:** Grant prospecting is under-AI-powered; most tools are database + search + filtering

### TheGrantScout Unique Position
- **Lower Price:** $99/month vs. $179 (Instrumentl), $150 (GrantStation), $55+/month (FDO subscription)
- **Specialized Focus:** 990-PF/foundation research only (not multi-source grant chaos)
- **AI Matching:** Semantic matching on mission + foundation preferences
- **Authority Data:** Real IRS data (not aggregated/third-party compiled)

### Competitive Messaging
Position as: **"Foundation Research for Every Nonprofit Budget"**
- Not competing on breadth (Instrumentl has federal/state/corporate)
- Competing on depth (143K foundations with rich history)
- Competing on affordability (accessible to small nonprofits)
- Competing on intelligence (AI matching vs. manual search)
