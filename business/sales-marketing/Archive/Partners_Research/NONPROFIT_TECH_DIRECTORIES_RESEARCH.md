# TheGrantScout - Nonprofit Tech Directories & Platforms Research

**Research Date:** December 22, 2025
**Purpose:** Identify 10+ nonprofit technology directories for listing TheGrantScout AI-powered grant matching service ($99/month)

---

## Summary

This research identifies **15 high-priority nonprofit tech directories and platforms** where TheGrantScout should be listed. These platforms reach nonprofits actively seeking grant management and fundraising solutions. The research covers major software review sites, nonprofit technology networks, grant-specific directories, and comparison platforms.

**Key Findings:**
- Most platforms offer **free basic listings** with optional paid/upgraded listings
- Listing approval typically takes **3-6 weeks**
- Major platforms (Capterra, G2, GetApp, Software Advice) share same parent company (Gartner Digital Markets)
- Grant-specific platforms have high engagement but lower traffic than general nonprofit software directories
- TechSoup partnership requires vetting but provides discount distribution channel
