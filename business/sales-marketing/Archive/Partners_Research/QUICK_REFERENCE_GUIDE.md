## Response Handling Quick Guide

**If They Respond Positively:**
→ Suggest 15-min calendar call (Calendly link)
→ Send demo account within 2 hours
→ Prepare: pitch deck + case study + customer examples

**If They Ask Questions:**
→ Answer within 4 hours (business hours)
→ Offer call if >2 back-and-forth emails
→ Have: feature list + pricing + case studies ready

**If They Say "Not Now":**
→ "Totally understand. What would change your mind? Q2? Q4?"
→ Add to "revisit in 6 months" list
→ Send quarterly updates on new features

**If They Say "No":**
→ "Thanks for considering. If you know anyone we should talk to, happy to explore partnerships with them."
→ Don't follow up again
→ Track reason in CRM

## Red Flags to Watch

🚩 Creator says "we don't recommend third-party tools"
→ Pivot to affiliate-only model

🚩 Creator wants large upfront payment
→ Start with affiliate; prove value first

🚩 Creator asks for equity
→ Decline unless strategic (rare)

🚩 Multiple follow-ups get no response
→ Stop reaching out; note and move on

🚩 Creator diverts to sales call
→ They're interested; have pricing ready

## Template Quick-Swap Sentences

**Energy Level - Adjust Tone:**
- High energy: "This could be a game-changer for your community"
- Medium energy: "We think your audience would find value in this"
- Low energy: "There may be opportunities to collaborate"

**Positioning - Swap Based on Creator:**
- For educators: "...help your students..."
- For founders: "...help the nonprofits you serve..."
- For institutions: "...enrich your curriculum..."

**Ask Size - Match to Creator Scale:**
- Large (80K+ subs): "Podcast guest + affiliate + integration"
- Medium (20-80K): "Podcast guest + affiliate"
- Smaller: "Affiliate only"
