## Contingency Notes

**If submission rejected:**
1. Request specific feedback on why
2. Revise and resubmit (most platforms allow appeals)
3. Ensure all requirements met (B2B, packaged solution, public trial offer, real screenshots)
4. Consider alternative positioning (e.g., "Grant Research Tool" vs. "Grant Prospecting Software")

**If partnership stalls:**
1. Follow up with decision-maker (don't let conversation die)
2. Offer pilot/test period instead of full commitment
3. Scale back scope (e.g., content partnership before data partnership)
4. Find alternative partnership routes (e.g., state councils vs. national for NCON)

---

## Important: Avoid These Mistakes

- ❌ Don't submit with generic/incomplete product info
- ❌ Don't use marketing language in official descriptions
- ❌ Don't skip providing screenshots/video demo materials
- ❌ Don't miss deadlines (especially conference submissions)
- ❌ Don't neglect competitor analysis (know your positioning vs. Instrumentl, GrantStation, etc.)
- ❌ Don't forget to claim profiles after approval (G2 requires active claim step)
- ❌ Don't ignore negative reviews (respond professionally, address legitimate feedback)
