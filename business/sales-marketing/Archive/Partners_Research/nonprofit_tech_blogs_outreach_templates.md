## General Tips for All Outreach

### Personalization Checklist
- [ ] Mention something specific from their work (blog post, podcast episode, recent article)
- [ ] Reference their specific audience or mission
- [ ] Explain WHY your tool is relevant to THEM (not just "you cover nonprofits")
- [ ] Show you understand their editorial focus

### Common Customizations by Outlet

**For Heather Mansfield (Nonprofit Tech for Good):**
- Emphasize digital fundraising and discovery
- Mention her X/Twitter influence and reach
- Position as "tool your 740k followers need to know about"

**For Beth Kanter:**
- Emphasize AI and "smart nonprofit" angle
- Reference her "Smart Nonprofit" book
- Position as innovation in nonprofit technology

**For NTEN (Amy Sample Ward):**
- Emphasize equity and access angle
- Note how AI can help underrepresented nonprofits find funding
- Mention their membership community benefits

**For NonProfit PRO (Amanda Cole):**
- Emphasize executive director / fundraiser audience
- Position as strategic fundraising insight
- Offer contributed article format

**For Nonprofit Tech Company Blogs:**
- Emphasize complementary tool positioning
- Show how users of their platform would benefit
- Offer co-marketing or exclusive offers

## Follow-Up Sequence

### If No Response Within 7 Days:
```
Hi [Name],

Following up on my email about [topic] from [date]. I realize you're likely busy, but wanted to check if you had a chance to review it.

Would you be open to a brief conversation about [specific opportunity]?

Thanks,
[Your Name]
```

### If Still No Response Within 14 Days:
```
Hi [Name],

One more quick follow-up—I completely understand if this isn't the right timing or fit.

If you'd ever like to explore [opportunity] in the future, I'm here. In the meantime, I'll continue following your work at [Outlet].

Best,
[Your Name]
```

### After 2 Follow-Ups:
Move to lower priority and revisit in 3 months, or explore alternative contacts at the organization.
