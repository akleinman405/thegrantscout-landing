## Quick Reference: What NOT to Say

❌ "We're better than [competitor]"
✅ "We solve different parts of the problem"

❌ "Your audience needs TheGrantScout"
✅ "Your audience would benefit from [specific outcome]"

❌ "Can we promote you?"
✅ "Can we create value together?"

❌ Generic: "I've been following your channel"
✅ Specific: "Your episode on [X] perfectly explained why grant research takes so long"

❌ "We have 143,000 foundations in our database"
✅ "We help nonprofits narrow 143,000 foundations down to 10 that actually want to fund them"

- **Email Open Rate Target:** 35%+ (grant writing niche is engaged)

- Send initial email Monday-Wednesday (9 AM your time)
- Follow up after 5 business days if no response
- Final follow-up after 10 business days
