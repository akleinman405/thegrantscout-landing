# PROMPT: PR & Influencer Outreach List

**Date:** 2025-12-22  
**For:** Research Team  
**Purpose:** Build comprehensive list of media contacts, influencers, and platforms for TheGrantScout PR outreach  
**Target:** 100+ contacts across 10 categories

---

## Situation

TheGrantScout is an AI-powered grant matching service for nonprofits ($99/month). We need to build awareness through earned media, influencer mentions, and platform listings. This list will be used for outreach starting January 2025.

**What TheGrantScout does:** Analyzes 143K+ foundations and 8.3M+ historical grants from IRS 990-PF data to find NEW foundation funding opportunities for nonprofits. Delivers monthly PDF reports with 5 curated matches, positioning guidance, and funder intelligence.

**Target audience:** Mid-market nonprofits ($500K-$5M budget) without dedicated grant staff.

---

## Task

Build a spreadsheet with 100+ contacts across 10 categories (minimum 10 per category):

### Categories (10+ each)

1. **Nonprofit Technology Blogs** - Sites reviewing/covering tech tools for nonprofits
2. **Grant Writing Blogs/Sites** - Grant writing tips, resources, coaching content
3. **Philanthropy Newsletters** - Email newsletters covering foundation/nonprofit sector
4. **Nonprofit Sector Podcasts** - Shows interviewing nonprofit leaders, covering sector trends
5. **LinkedIn Influencers** - Individual voices with 10K+ followers in nonprofit/philanthropy space
6. **Twitter/X Voices** - Active accounts covering nonprofit tech, grants, philanthropy
7. **YouTube Channels** - Nonprofit management, grant writing, fundraising content
8. **Trade Publications** - Chronicle of Philanthropy, NonProfit Times, etc.
9. **Grant Writing Coaches/Consultants** - Individuals with audiences who might refer clients
10. **Nonprofit Tech Directories/Platforms** - Sites where TheGrantScout should be listed

---

## Required Fields

For each contact, capture:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Category | Which of the 10 categories |
| Company/Outlet | Publication, podcast, platform name |
| Name | Individual contact name (if applicable) |
| Title/Role | Their position |
| Bio | 1-2 sentence description of who they are/what they cover |
| Audience Size | Followers, subscribers, monthly visitors (estimate OK) |
| Recent Relevant Content | Link to article/episode showing they cover this space |
| Positioning Notes | Why TheGrantScout is relevant to their audience, pitch angle |
| Email | Direct email if findable |
| LinkedIn | Profile URL |
| Twitter/X | Handle |
| Other Contact | Submission form, DM preference, etc. |
| Recommended Method | Best way to reach out (email, LinkedIn, Twitter DM, submission form) |
| Priority | High/Medium/Low based on audience fit and reachability |

## Quality Checks

- [ ] Recent relevant content link confirms they actually cover this space
- [ ] Positioning notes are specific (not generic)
- [ ] Priority ratings are defensible

## Notes

- Prioritize people/outlets that specifically cover grant seeking, foundation funding, or nonprofit revenue - not just general nonprofit content
- Look for smaller, engaged audiences over huge generic ones
- Grant writing coaches are high value - they have direct relationships with our target customers
- For directories, note any listing fees or requirements
