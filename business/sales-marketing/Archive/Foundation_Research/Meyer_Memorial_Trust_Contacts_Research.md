## KEY INSIGHTS FOR OUTREACH

### Why Meyer is Ideal for Grant Discovery Tool Pilot

1. **Grantmaking Scale:** 350 intermediary grants annually means significant portfolio of grantees who likely need funding intelligence
2. **Strategic Positioning:** New VP of Impact (Kim Melton) is positioned to innovate grantee support
3. **Demonstrated Interest in Grantee Success:** Created new Impact Department specifically to implement trust-based practices and learn from grantees
4. **Community-Centered Approach:** Recent hire of three senior program officers with deep community organizing backgrounds suggests investment in grantee relationships
5. **Policy Focus:** Multi-program officers with policy expertise would understand funding infrastructure gaps

### Recommended Outreach Strategy

**Tier 1 Contact:** Nancy Haque (Director of Policy & Programs)
- Closest to day-to-day grantee relationships
- Oversees programs affecting all 350 grantees
- Most likely to champion tools that reduce grantee burden

**Tier 2 Contact:** Kimberly Melton (VP of Impact)
- Strategic decision-maker for new grantee support initiatives
- Can authorize pilot programs or expanded grantee services
- Background in journalism/communications means data-driven approach to solutions

**Messaging Angle:**
