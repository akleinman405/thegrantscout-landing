## Part 6: Methodology & Limitations

### Database Approach
1. **Address Clustering:** Identified addresses with 5+ private foundations (42 addresses found)
2. **Officer Analysis:** Searched officers table for entities with "Trust," "Bank," "Foundation Services" appearing as trustees (116 entities found)

### Web Research
1. Searched for known foundation management companies
2. Verified headquarters, services, and scale
3. Cross-referenced with database findings

### Limitations
- Some management relationships not captured in IRS filings (registered agent addresses vs. actual manager)
- Management company names sometimes abbreviated differently
- Some foundations use family addresses rather than manager addresses
- Fiscal year timing means some data is 1-2 years old
