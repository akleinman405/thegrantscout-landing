## 8. Methodology Notes

### Search Patterns Used

1. **177 E Colorado Blvd, Pasadena** (Whittier Trust HQ)
   - Matched: 25 foundations
   - Variations: "177 E Colorado", "177 East Colorado", "Suite 800", "8th FL"

2. **100 W Liberty Street, Reno** (Whittier Trust Reno Office)
   - Matched: 11 foundations
   - Suites: 100, 750, 770, 890, 1100, 10th Floor

3. **505 Montgomery Street, San Francisco** (Whittier Trust SF Office)
   - Matched: 3 foundations
   - Suites: 620, 1200, 7th Floor

4. **Officer/Trustee Name Search**
   - "Whittier Trust Company" or "Whittier Trust" in officer name or title
   - Found 3 additional foundations not at standard addresses

### Limitations

- **Family Addresses:** Some Whittier-managed foundations may use family addresses instead of Whittier office addresses - these would not be captured
- **Other Offices:** Did not find foundations at Newport Beach, Menlo Park, Seattle, or Portland office addresses
- **Pegine Grayson:** Searched for "Grayson" in officers but results were mostly unrelated family foundations (Bradley Grayson, Donna Grayson, etc.) - did not find Pegine specifically
- **Data Recency:** Most recent filings are 2023-2024; some older filings may show different addresses

### Confidence Level

- **High confidence:** 25 Pasadena HQ foundations (distinctive address)
- **High confidence:** 11 Reno office foundations (distinctive address)
- **Medium confidence:** 3 SF office foundations (505 Montgomery is a commercial building with other tenants)
- **High confidence:** 3 officer-match foundations (explicit "Whittier Trust" in records)
