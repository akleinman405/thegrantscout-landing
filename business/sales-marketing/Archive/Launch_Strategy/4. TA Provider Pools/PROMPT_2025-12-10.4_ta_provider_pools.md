# 🧠 RESEARCH TEAM TASK
## State TA Provider Pools — Get Paid by States to Serve Nonprofits

**Date:** 2025-12-10  
**Purpose:** Identify state-level Technical Assistance (TA) Provider Pools where Alec can get approved as a vendor — then get paid directly by the state to serve nonprofits.

---

## 🎯 Mission

Find state programs where:
1. Alec applies and gets approved as a "capacity-building provider"
2. Nonprofits receive vouchers/grants for capacity building
3. Nonprofits hire Alec from the approved list
4. **State pays Alec directly** — nonprofit pays nothing

This is the closest model to "automatic reimbursement" in the nonprofit sector.

---

## What Are TA Provider Pools?

States maintain approved vendor lists of "Capacity-Building Providers" or "Technical Assistance Providers." When a nonprofit receives a capacity-building grant/voucher, they hire vendors from this pre-approved list.

**The model:**
```
1. State creates TA Provider Pool with funding
2. Alec applies, gets approved as "grant readiness" vendor
3. Nonprofits receive capacity vouchers
4. Nonprofits pick TheGrantScout from the list
5. Alec delivers service
6. State pays Alec directly
```

**Why this matters:** Once approved, Alec can proactively reach out to voucher recipients: "Your grant covers my service at no cost to you."

---

## Where TA Provider Pools Live

They're scattered across different agencies:
- State nonprofit associations
- State economic development offices
- State community development agencies
- AmeriCorps state commissions
- State departments of commerce
- Governor's office of community initiatives

## Information to Capture

### Program Details
- Program name
- Administering agency
- Website URL
- Application page URL
- Services covered (is "grant readiness" or "fundraising support" included?)
- Payment structure (hourly, per engagement, per nonprofit?)
- Typical engagement size ($)

### Application Process
- Is enrollment currently open?
- Application deadline
- Requirements (credentials, insurance, etc.)
- Approval timeline
- How long does approval last?

### Outreach Strategy (Critical)
- **Are voucher recipient lists public?** Can we find out which nonprofits received capacity-building grants?
- **When are capacity grants awarded?** What's the cycle timing so we can outreach right after?
- **Will agency share recipient info with approved providers?** Can we request the list directly?
- **Does agency send "available providers" list to recipients?** Can we get included?
- **Where to monitor for award announcements?** Press releases, newsletters?
- **Co-marketing opportunities?** Joint emails, inclusion in materials?
