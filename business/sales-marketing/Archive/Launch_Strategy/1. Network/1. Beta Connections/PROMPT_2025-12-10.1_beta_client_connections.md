## Part 1: F990 Database Research

### Step 1: Find Beta Client EINs

EINs are already known:

| Organization | EIN |
|--------------|-----|
| Patient Safety Movement Foundation | 462730379 |
| Retirement Housing Foundation | 952249495 |
| Senior Network Services | 942259716 |
| Ka Ulukoa | 260542078 |
| Arborbrook Christian Academy | 202707577 |
| Horizons National | 61468129 |
| Friendship Circle SD | 203472700 |

Verify they appear in the grants data:

```sql
-- Verify beta clients appear in grants data
SELECT DISTINCT 
    recipient_name_raw,
    recipient_ein,
    recipient_state,
    recipient_city,
    COUNT(*) as grant_count,
    SUM(amount) as total_received
FROM f990_2025.fact_grants
WHERE recipient_ein IN ('462730379', '952249495', '942259716', '260542078', '202707577', '61468129', '203472700')
GROUP BY recipient_name_raw, recipient_ein, recipient_state, recipient_city
ORDER BY total_received DESC;
```

### Step 2: Find Who Funded Each Beta Client

```sql
-- Find all foundations that have funded beta clients
SELECT 
    fg.recipient_name_raw AS beta_client,
    fg.recipient_ein AS beta_client_ein,
    df.name AS funder_name,
    df.ein AS funder_ein,
    df.city AS funder_city,
    df.state AS funder_state,
    fg.amount,
    fg.purpose_text,
    fg.tax_year
FROM f990_2025.fact_grants fg
JOIN f990_2025.dim_foundations df ON fg.foundation_ein = df.ein
WHERE fg.recipient_ein IN ('462730379', '952249495', '942259716', '260542078', '202707577', '61468129', '203472700')
ORDER BY fg.recipient_name_raw, fg.tax_year DESC, fg.amount DESC;
```

### Step 3: Find Other Grantees of Beta Client Funders

```sql
-- Find peer organizations (other grantees of beta clients' funders)
WITH beta_funders AS (
    SELECT DISTINCT foundation_ein
    FROM f990_2025.fact_grants
    WHERE recipient_ein IN ('462730379', '952249495', '942259716', '260542078', '202707577', '61468129', '203472700')
)
SELECT 
    df.name AS funder_name,
    df.ein AS funder_ein,
    fg.recipient_name_raw AS peer_organization,
    fg.recipient_ein AS peer_ein,
    fg.recipient_state,
    fg.amount,
    fg.purpose_text,
    fg.tax_year
FROM f990_2025.fact_grants fg
JOIN f990_2025.dim_foundations df ON fg.foundation_ein = df.ein
JOIN beta_funders bf ON fg.foundation_ein = bf.foundation_ein
WHERE fg.recipient_ein NOT IN ('462730379', '952249495', '942259716', '260542078', '202707577', '61468129', '203472700')
ORDER BY df.name, fg.tax_year DESC;
```

### Step 3b: Find Known Funders from Questionnaire

```sql
-- Find known funders and their other grantees
SELECT 
    df.name AS funder_name,
```
