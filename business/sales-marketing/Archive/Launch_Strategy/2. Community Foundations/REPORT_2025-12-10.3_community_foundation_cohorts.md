## Recommended Pitch Approaches

### Angle A - Plug into existing program (28 foundations)
> "I see you run [Program Name]. I specialize in grant discovery - could I serve your cohort participants?"

**Best for:** Foundations with active cohorts (New Haven, Omaha, Ozarks, Orange County)

### Angle B - Propose a new program (15 foundations)
> "For $12K, I'll provide grant discovery services to 10 of your grantees for a full year. You fund it once, they get ongoing support."

**Best for:** Large foundations without structured cohorts (SVCF, California CF, Greater Houston)

### Angle C - Webinar partnership (7 foundations)
> "I'd love to offer a free webinar for your grantees on finding foundation funding."

**Best for:** Foundations with workshop series (Oklahoma City, Jackson Hole)
