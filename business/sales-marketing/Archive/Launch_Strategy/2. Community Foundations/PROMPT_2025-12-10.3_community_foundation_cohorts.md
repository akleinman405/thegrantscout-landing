# 🧠 RESEARCH TEAM TASK
## Community Foundation Cohorts — B2B Partnership Opportunities

**Date:** 2025-12-10  
**Purpose:** Build a list of community foundations that run capacity-building cohorts or grantee support programs — and identify opportunities to plug TheGrantScout in as a service provider.

---

## 🎯 Mission

Identify community foundations where Alec can pitch TheGrantScout as a service provider to their grantees. The goal is B2B deals where the foundation pays Alec directly to serve 10-20+ nonprofits.

---

## What We're Looking For

Community foundations often run programs like:
- "Nonprofit capacity-building cohorts"
- "Grant readiness programs"
- "Grantee support services"
- "Emerging leaders cohorts"
- "Strategic planning support"

These programs either:
- **Hire vendors directly** to serve their grantees (Alec gets paid by foundation)
- **Fund nonprofits** who then hire vendors (Alec gets paid by nonprofit with foundation money)

**The deal:** "Fund grant discovery for your grantees. $1,200/org/year × 10 orgs = $12K. I handle everything."

---

## Research Process

### Step 1: Build Target List (F990)

Query community foundations with $10M+ assets:

```sql
SELECT 
    name,
    ein,
    state,
    city,
    total_assets,
    total_giving
FROM f990_2025.dim_foundations
WHERE name ILIKE '%community foundation%'
  AND total_assets > 10000000
ORDER BY total_assets DESC
LIMIT 200;
```

### Step 2: Website Research (Top 50)

For the top 50 by assets, check their website for capacity programs.

**Programs to look for:**
- "Capacity building grants"
- "Nonprofit support"
- "Grantee services"
- "Technical assistance"
- "Grant readiness"
- "Cohort programs"
- "Nonprofit training"
- "Organizational development grants"

**Information to capture:**
- Program name
- Program description
- Is it currently active?
- Application deadline (if nonprofits apply)
- Do they hire vendors/consultants?
- Program officer name and contact
- Number of nonprofits served per cohort

---

## Research Questions

For each foundation:

1. Does this foundation run any nonprofit support programs?
2. Is there an active cohort or training program?
3. Do they contract with external vendors/consultants?
4. Who is the program officer or contact?
5. What's the timing? (rolling, annual cycle, specific deadlines)
6. How many nonprofits do they serve per program?
7. Is there a way to propose a new program?

## Program Types to Tag

- `cohort` — Structured multi-org program
- `grants` — Capacity grants to individual nonprofits
- `training` — Workshops/webinars
- `vendor_services` — They contract vendors to serve grantees
- `grantee_support` — General support for their grantees

---

## Pitch Angles

For each foundation, note which pitch makes sense:

### Angle A — Plug into existing program
> "I see you run [Program Name]. I specialize in grant discovery — could I serve your cohort participants?"

### Angle B — Propose a new program
> "For $12K, I'll provide grant discovery services to 10 of your grantees for a full year. You fund it once, they get ongoing support."

### Angle C — Webinar partnership
> "I'd love to offer a free webinar for your grantees on finding foundation funding."

## Priority Scoring (1-10)

Score each foundation based on:

| Factor | Weight |
|--------|--------|
| Has active capacity program | 3 pts |
| Hires external vendors | 2 pts |
| Program timing aligns (upcoming cohort) | 2 pts |
| Contact info available | 1 pt |
| Geographic fit (CA, TX, etc.) | 1 pt |
| Foundation size ($50M+ assets) | 1 pt |
