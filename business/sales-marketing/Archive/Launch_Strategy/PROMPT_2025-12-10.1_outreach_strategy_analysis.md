## 📊 PRIORITIZATION FRAMEWORK

For each avenue, evaluate and score:

### 1. Impact Potential (0-30)
- How many qualified leads could this generate?
- What's the likely conversion rate?
- What's the revenue potential per lead?

### 2. Effort Required (0-25)
- Time to first results (days/weeks/months)
- Ongoing maintenance burden
- Skills/resources required

### 3. Dependencies (0-20)
- What needs to exist first? (product, case studies, integrations)
- External approvals or partnerships required?
- Seasonal timing considerations?

### 4. Strategic Value (0-15)
- Brand/credibility building beyond direct sales?
- Learning value (customer insights, market validation)?
- Compounds over time?

### 5. Cost (0-10)
- Direct costs (ads, fees, travel)
- Opportunity cost of Alec's time

**Total = 100 points**

Invert effort and cost scores so higher = better (low effort, low cost = high score).
