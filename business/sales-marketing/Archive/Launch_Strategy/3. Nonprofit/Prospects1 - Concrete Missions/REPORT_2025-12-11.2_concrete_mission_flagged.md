## 10. Recommendations

### Target Lists for Outreach

| List | Criteria | Count | Use Case |
|------|----------|-------|----------|
| **Prime** | Concrete + Capacity + ICP 8+ | 642 | Cold calls, high-value outreach |
| **Warm** | Concrete + ICP 8+ | 2,378 | Email campaigns, events |
| **Volume** | Concrete + Capacity | 3,287 | Scaled outreach |
| **Full** | All concrete mission | 7,277 | Brand awareness |

### Recommended SQL for Prime List

```sql
SELECT ein, org_name, sector, employee_count, icp_score,
       website, mission_statement,
       capacity_grant_amount, capacity_grant_funder_name
FROM f990_2025.prospects
WHERE has_concrete_mission = 1
  AND received_capacity_grant = 1
  AND icp_score >= 8
ORDER BY icp_score DESC, capacity_grant_amount DESC NULLS LAST;
```

### Why Not Use Golden Overlap?

The golden overlap (Concrete + Reachable + Capacity) yields only **9 prospects** because:
- reach_all_flags requires employee count 1-10, which conflicts with high-clarity range (16-50)
- reach_all_flags is very restrictive (only 290 total)

**Better approach:** Drop reachability requirement, use capacity grant + ICP as quality signals instead.
