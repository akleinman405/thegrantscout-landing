## Executive Summary

This analysis examines cold call data from TheGrantScout's beta outreach campaign to identify patterns that predict successfully reaching decision makers and generating interest. The key finding is that **mid-sized nonprofits ($1M-$2M revenue) in the Healthcare (E) and Human Services (P) sectors with high grant dependency (90-100%) show the highest connection and interest rates**.

**Key Metrics:**
- **28% connection rate** (14 of 50 calls reached someone)
- **8% decision maker rate** (4 of 50 calls reached the right person)
- **6% interest rate** (3 orgs expressed Yes or Maybe)
- **75% conversion from DM to interest** (when you reach the right person, 3 of 4 showed interest)

## Key Findings

1. **Mid-sized nonprofits convert best:** Organizations in the $1M-$2M revenue range show highest connection (33%) and interest rates. They're large enough to need grant help but small enough to answer the phone.

2. **Healthcare and Human Services sectors show strongest interest:** NTEE sectors E (Healthcare) and P (Human Services) account for all 3 interested organizations, despite Education (B) having the most calls.

3. **High grant dependency = reachability:** Organizations with 90-100% grant dependency have the highest connection rates (35.7%), likely because they have dedicated development staff who answer calls.

4. **Decision maker = conversion:** When you reach the decision maker, 75% express interest. The challenge is getting through, not convincing them.

5. **Wednesday is the best call day:** 35.7% connection rate vs 14-21% on other days.

6. **Beta clients came from this list:** PSMF (Maybe) and RHF/Anaheim Memorial Manor (Yes) both converted to paying beta clients, validating the ICP.

## Targeting Recommendations

### For Future Cold Calls
1. **Prioritize $1M-$2M revenue band** — highest connection and interest rates
2. **Focus on Healthcare (E) and Human Services (P) sectors** — all conversions came from these sectors
3. **Target 75-100% grant dependency** — these orgs have development staff
4. **Call on Wednesdays** — 35.7% connection rate vs 14-21% other days

### For Email Campaigns
Filter F990 database using these ICP criteria:
- Revenue: $1,400,000 - $2,100,000
- Grant dependency: 72% - 98%
- NTEE codes starting with E or P
- Start with California (proven market), expand to other states

### Avoid
- Organizations with >100% or <25% grant dependency (low connection, zero interest)
- Large organizations (>$3M revenue) — harder to reach, less interested
- Pure Education (B) sector — high call volume but zero conversions to interest

## Database Query for ICP Prospects

```sql
-- Find F990 nonprofits matching ICP criteria
SELECT
    ein, organization_name, state,
    total_revenue,
    ntee_code,
    ROUND(100.0 * grants_revenue / NULLIF(total_revenue, 0), 1) as grant_dependency_pct
FROM f990_2025.nonprofit_returns
WHERE total_revenue BETWEEN 1400000 AND 2100000
  AND ntee_code LIKE 'E%' OR ntee_code LIKE 'P%'
  AND grants_revenue > 0
  AND (grants_revenue::float / NULLIF(total_revenue, 0)) BETWEEN 0.72 AND 0.98
ORDER BY state, total_revenue DESC;
```
