# Community Foundation Capacity Building Programs - Organized by Type

## COHORT-BASED FORMAL TRAINING PROGRAMS

### 1. South Dakota Community Foundation - 15-Month Credential Program
- **Program:** South Dakota Nonprofit Capacity Building Program
- **Duration:** 15 months
- **Credential:** Certificate in Fund Raising Management (CFRM) + Certified Fund Raising Executive (CFRE) requirements
- **Partners:** Fund Raising School at Indiana University Lilly Family School of Philanthropy; CFRE International
- **Covers:** Nonprofit fundraising, leadership skills, ethical fundraising practices
- **Contact:** info@SDCommunityFoundation.org | 800.888.1842
- **Cost:** Information not specified (likely subsidized)
- **Status:** Active

### 2. Community Foundation for Greater Buffalo - Fund for the Arts Cohorts
- **Program:** Fund for the Arts Cohort-Based Capacity Building; Ralph C. Wilson Jr. Arts & Culture Initiative
- **Duration:** Multi-year cohorts (specific length not detailed)
- **Focus:** ARTS SECTOR ONLY
- **Includes:**
  - Capacity-Building + General Operating Grants (for orgs with 1+ FTE staff)
  - Micro Grants (for orgs with <1 FTE staff)
  - Regional cultural planning initiatives
- **Contact:** yukinr@cfgb.org (Yuki Numata Resnick, Director Arts & Culture)
- **Training:** Virtual grant training sessions offered (e.g., June 2025)
- **Status:** Active

## GRANTS FOR CAPACITY BUILDING

### 5. Arkansas Community Foundation - Giving Tree Grant Program
- **Program:** Giving Tree Grants focused on capacity building
- **Grant Amounts:** Not specified (Varies by project)
- **Application Period:** July 10 - August 15 (ANNUAL DEADLINE)
- **Distributed Through:** 29 affiliate offices statewide
- **Typical Capacity Projects:**
  - Equipment/computers/databases/financial systems
  - Facility upgrades
  - Communications/marketing consulting, branding, social media, e-news campaigns
  - Evaluation system development
  - Shared services (IT support, back-office admin, financial services)
  - Startup assistance

- **Contact:** arcf@arcf.org | 501-372-1116
- **Status:** Active

### 6. South Dakota Community Foundation - Beyond Idea Grants (BIG)
- **Program:** Beyond Idea Grant (BIG) Program (Partnership: Bush Foundation)
- **Grant Range:** $5,000 - $100,000 per year ($50K max for development phase; $100K max for testing/spreading)
- **Duration:** 6-year initiative with $1.2M distributed annually
- **Focus:** Community-based problem solving; equitable change leadership
- **Application:** Competitive process (dates vary by year)
- **Contact:** info@SDCommunityFoundation.org | 800.888.1842
- **Status:** Active

### 7. Hawaii Community Foundation - FLEX Grants Program
- **Program:** FLEX Grants (unrestricted support for high-performing nonprofits)
- **Two Tracks:**
  - Organizations with $100K+ annual operating budget
  - Organizations with <$100K annual operating budget

- **Example Impact:** 2019: $4M distributed to 21 nonprofits with multiyear support
- **Contact:** info@hcf-hawaii.org | (808) 537-6333
- **Status:** Active

### 8. Community Foundation of Middle Tennessee - Agency Endowment Funds
- **Program:** Support for nonprofits establishing organizational endowments
- **Purpose:** Long-term financial stability; permanent funding source
- **Benefit:** CFMT manages investment oversight/reporting; nonprofit stays focused on mission
- **Contact:** givingback@cfmt.org | 615-321-4939
- **Status:** Active

### 9. Arkansas Community Foundation - Organizational Endowment Support
- **Program:** Organizational endowments with ARCF management
- **Purpose:** Create permanent annual income for operations and programs
- **Benefit:** ARCF handles investment oversight, management, reporting
- **Availability:** Through 29 affiliate offices statewide
- **Contact:** arcf@arcf.org | 501-372-1116
- **Status:** Active

## TECHNOLOGY & OPERATIONAL SUPPORT SERVICES

### 10. Tulsa Community Foundation - 501TechNet
- **Program:** 501TechNet (Nonprofit Technology Assistance)
- **Service Model:** Technology consulting + volume-based purchasing agreements
- **Cost:** Services available at 25-50% of market cost
- **Services:** Technology infrastructure, systems, consulting
- **Partners:** Strategic technology vendors (named partners have volume agreements)
- **Contact:** info@tulsacf.org | 918-494-8823
- **Status:** Active
- **Note:** One of largest community foundations in nation; donor-advised model (no unsolicited grant requests)

- **Contact:** reservations@marincf.org | 415-464-2500
- **Status:** Active
