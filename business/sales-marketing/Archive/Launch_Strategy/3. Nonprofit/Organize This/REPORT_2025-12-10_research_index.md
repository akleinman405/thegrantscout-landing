1. **Marin Community Foundation** (California)
   - Strongest program: Community Power Initiative ($30M, 3-year)
   - Best for: Multi-year comprehensive support

2. **Greater Houston Community Foundation** (Texas)
   - Strongest program: Foundation Services + education
   - Best for: Donor education; large-scale grantmaking

3. **Hawaii Community Foundation** (Hawaii)
   - Strongest programs: Travel scholarships, FLEX grants, Resilience Fund TA
   - Best for: Geographic barriers; equitable support

4. **Tulsa Community Foundation** (Oklahoma)
   - Strongest program: 501TechNet (25-50% cost services)
   - Best for: Technology support

5. **Community Foundation of Middle Tennessee** (Tennessee)
   - Strongest programs: Multi-service support; free spaces; fiscal sponsorship
   - Best for: Comprehensive nonprofit services

6. **South Dakota Community Foundation** (South Dakota)
   - Strongest program: 15-month CFRM/CFRE credential program
   - Best for: Formal training; international credentials

7. **Community Foundation for Greater Buffalo** (New York)
   - Strongest program: Cohort-based capacity building for arts
   - Best for: Arts organizations; collaborative learning

8. **Community Foundation of New Jersey** (New Jersey)
   - Strongest program: Large-scale grantmaking ($100M+ annually)
   - Best for: Funding distribution; related regional TA

9. **Oklahoma City Community Foundation** (Oklahoma)
   - Strongest program: Quarterly endowment training workshops
   - Best for: Endowment building; ongoing training

10. **Arkansas Community Foundation** (Arkansas)
    - Strongest programs: Giving Tree grants, endowment support, Black philanthropy
    - Best for: Diverse capacity needs; equity focus

## RESEARCH METHODOLOGY

### Sources Used
- Foundation official websites (direct fetch)
- Web searches (Google-based)
- Specific program pages (targeted fetch)
- Web search results (news, directories, databases)

### Verification Approach
- Cross-referenced information across multiple pages
- Verified URLs and phone numbers
- Noted when information was incomplete or required direct contact

### Data Quality Assessment
- **High confidence:** Program names, foundation addresses, basic contact info
- **Medium confidence:** Program descriptions, capacity served, deadline dates
- **Requires verification:** Specific eligibility, current enrollment numbers, vendor rates
