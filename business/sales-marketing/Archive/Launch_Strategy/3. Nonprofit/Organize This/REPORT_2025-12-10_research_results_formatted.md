## SUMMARY FINDINGS

### Programs by Category

**Most Comprehensive Programs:**
1. San Antonio Area Foundation - Multiple certificates, cohorts, workshops
2. Columbus Foundation - Structured programs with multiple entry points
3. East Bay Community Foundation - Specialized accelerator model

**Growing Programs:**
1. Central Indiana Community Foundation - New director, new initiatives
2. Community Foundation of Louisville - Expanding cohort and grant programs

**Limited Information Programs:**
1. Community Foundation of Greater Birmingham
2. Community Foundation of Greater Chattanooga
3. Lincoln Community Foundation
4. Wichita Community Foundation
5. Rochester Area Community Foundation (moderate visibility)

### Vendor Hiring Status

**Confirmed Active Vendor Hiring:**
- San Antonio Area Foundation
- Columbus Foundation
- East Bay Community Foundation
- Community Foundation of Louisville
- Central Indiana Community Foundation

**Unknown:**
- Rochester Area Community Foundation
- Lincoln Community Foundation
- Wichita Community Foundation
- Community Foundation of Greater Birmingham
- Community Foundation of Greater Chattanooga

### Overall Assessment

8 of 10 foundations (80%) have identified capacity-building programs. Of those with programs, 5 explicitly hire external vendors and consultants, indicating active market for nonprofit development services. Community foundations increasingly view capacity-building as distinct from grantmaking and are investing in professional expertise to serve this mission.
