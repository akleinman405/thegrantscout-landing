## Optimizations Made

### 1. Supabase Integration
- Pulls prospects directly from Supabase (website not null, email is null)
- Updates prospects in Supabase with scraped emails
- Filters out N/A, NONE, 0 website values

### 2. Quality Filters
Added exclusions for false positive emails:
- Grant portals: `@grantstation`, `@foundant`, `@fluxx`, `@submittable`
- Platform emails: `@wix`, `@squarespace`, `@wordpress`
- Generic: `noreply@`, `careers@`, `hr@`, `webmaster@`

### 3. URL Filtering
Skip non-nonprofit URLs:
- Job sites: `icims.com`, `workday.com`, `greenhouse.io`
- Social: `linkedin.com`, `facebook.com`, `twitter.com`
- Grant portals: `grantstation.com`, `foundant.com`

### 4. Performance
- Reduced timeout from 20s to 12s
- Optimized concurrency (15-20 connections)
- Speed: 2.0 sites/second (vs 0.2 before optimization)

### 5. Domain Matching
- Emails from matching domains get +100 score
- Emails from non-matching domains get -50 score
- Only positive-score emails marked as "high" confidence
