**Funding Range:** $25,000 - $150,000 annually
**Cycle:** Same RFP cycle as Title III-B
**Fit Score:** 85/100 - STRONG
**Status:** EXPANSION OPPORTUNITY

#### Program Description
The National Family Caregiver Support Program (NFCSP) provides grants to support family and informal caregivers caring for older adults at home.

#### Eligible Services
- Information to caregivers about available services
- Assistance gaining access to services
- Individual counseling for caregiving decisions
- Caregiver training for problem-solving
- Respite care (temporary relief from caregiving)
- Supplemental services to complement caregiver-provided care
- Support groups
- Translation services

#### Spending Limitations

- Must coordinate with California Caregiver Resource Centers (CCRCs)

#### Evidence-Based Programs to Consider
- Powerful Tools for Caregivers
- REACH II (Resources for Enhancing Alzheimer's Caregiver Health)
- Savvy Caregiver
- TCARE (Tailored Caregiver Assessment and Referral)

#### Positioning Strategy
Highlight any existing caregiver support experience and willingness to implement evidence-based programs. Consider partnership with CCRC for comprehensive approach.

## Appendix B: Glossary

**AAA:** Area Agency on Aging - Local agencies that plan and coordinate services for older adults under the Older Americans Act

**ADRC:** Aging and Disability Resource Connection - No Wrong Door system for accessing long-term services and supports

**CalAIM:** California Advancing and Innovating Medi-Cal - Statewide initiative transforming Medi-Cal delivery

**CBO:** Community-Based Organization

**CCRC:** California Caregiver Resource Centers

**CDA:** California Department of Aging

**CSNSGP:** California State Nonprofit Security Grant Program

**ECM:** Enhanced Care Management - CalAIM benefit for high-need Medi-Cal members

**HICAP:** Health Insurance Counseling and Advocacy Program

**I&A:** Information and Assistance

**ILC:** Independent Living Center

**LOI:** Letter of Inquiry

**MCP:** Managed Care Plan

**NWD:** No Wrong Door - System design principle for seamless service access

**OAA:** Older Americans Act

**OCA:** Older Californians Act

**PSA:** Planning and Service Area - Geographic region for AAA services

**RFP:** Request for Proposals
