**Amount:** $10,000 - $200,000
**Deadline:** January 5, 2026
**FACILITY ELIGIBLE:** YES - PROVEN SUCCESS
**Fit Score:** 95/100
**Contact:** Elizabeth Lentz-Hill, (808) 566-5543, elentzhill@hcf-hawaii.org

**Application Process:**

3. For projects >$500K: Document 60% funding secured
4. Submit capital request (buildings, equipment, vehicles)

- Capital requests ONLY accepted at July deadline (except $25K or less)

## Section 7: Success Metrics & Positioning Intelligence

### Key Success Factors

**Native Hawaiian Emphasis (Critical for Multiple Funders)**
- Document % of Native Hawaiian youth served
- Incorporate cultural practices in programming
- Use appropriate Hawaiian language terminology
- Partner with Native Hawaiian organizations
- Include cultural advisors on board/staff

**Facility as Community Asset**
- Public access provisions
- Community event hosting capability
- Disaster relief/resilience function
- Multi-generational programming potential
- Economic development impact

**Evidence-Based Approach**
- Academic achievement correlation
- Crime prevention statistics
- Health outcome improvements
- College readiness metrics
- Career pathway development

### Winning Narrative Elements

1. **The Precedent:** "Following Mid-Pacific School's successful $200K Atherton grant for track facilities"

2. **The Need:** "X% of Hawaii youth lack access to quality athletic facilities"

3. **The Culture:** "Perpetuating Native Hawaiian values through athletics and mentorship"

4. **The Impact:** "Serving XXX low-income youth annually with free programming"

5. **The Sustainability:** "Facility rental revenue will support ongoing operations"

### Common Funder Concerns & Responses

**"How will you maintain the facility?"**
- Detailed maintenance budget
- Revenue generation plan
- Volunteer engagement strategy
- Partner organization commitments

**"Why not use existing facilities?"**
- Document current facility limitations
- Show scheduling conflicts
- Demonstrate cost savings long-term
- Highlight unique programming needs

**"What about operational funding?"**
- Diversified funding model
- Earned revenue projections
- Individual donor pipeline
- Corporate sponsorship plan
