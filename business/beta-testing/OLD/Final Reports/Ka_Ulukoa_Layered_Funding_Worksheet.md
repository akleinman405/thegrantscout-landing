## About This Worksheet

This strategic planning worksheet helps Ka Ulukoa execute a multi-funder "layered funding" approach to facility acquisition, following the proven model used by organizations like Mid-Pacific School (which secured $200,000 from Atherton Family Foundation for their track facility in 2024). The worksheet breaks your total facility funding need into four strategic layers: Anchor Grant (40-60%), Secondary Major Gift (20-30%), Multiple Small Grants (10-20%), and Match/In-Kind Contributions (5-10%). Use this worksheet to plan your application sequence, track funding progress, and ensure you meet funders' requirements such as Atherton's 60% rule. Update this worksheet bi-weekly as you submit applications and receive funding decisions.

## Atherton 60% Rule - Critical Planning Section

### What is the 60% Rule?

For capital projects exceeding $500,000 total cost, Atherton Family Foundation requires documentation that 60% of project funding is already secured before they will consider an award.

### Does This Apply to Ka Ulukoa?

| If Total Project Is... | 60% Threshold | Rule Applies? |
|------------------------|---------------|---------------|
| Under $500,000 | N/A | NO |
| $500,000 | $300,000 | YES |
| $750,000 | $450,000 | YES |
| $1,000,000 | $600,000 | YES |
| $1,500,000 | $900,000 | YES |

### Your 60% Calculation

| Field | Amount |
|-------|--------|
| **Total Project Cost** | $ _____________ |
| **60% Threshold** | $ _____________ (multiply above by 0.6) |
| **Current Secured Funding** | $ _____________ |
| **Gap to 60%** | $ _____________ |

### Strategy if 60% Rule Applies

**Option A: Request under $500K total project**
- Structure initial request for first phase only
- Keep total project under threshold
- Return for subsequent phases later

**Option B: Secure 60% first**
- Prioritize other funders before Atherton
- Use Bank of Hawaii, Weinberg, LWCF as building blocks
- Apply to Atherton once 60% threshold met

**Option C: Phased approach**
- Request initial Atherton grant for project component
- Build toward 60% threshold
- Request additional Atherton support in future year

### Sequencing for 60% Compliance

If your project exceeds $500K, consider this modified sequence:

| Order | Funder | Target | Running Total |
|-------|--------|--------|---------------|
| 1 | Bank of Hawaii | $50,000 | $50,000 |
