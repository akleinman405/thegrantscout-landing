## 10. SPECIFIC RECOMMENDATIONS FOR ENHANCEMENT

### Priority 1 - Immediate Fixes

1. **Add Executive Dashboard** to all reports:
   - Total opportunities: X
   - Potential funding: $X-Y
   - Urgent deadlines: X
   - Success probability: High/Medium/Low
   - Effort required: Hours estimated

2. **Standardize Visual Alerts:**
   - 🔴 URGENT (< 30 days)
   - 🟡 HIGH PRIORITY (30-60 days)
   - 🟢 MEDIUM PRIORITY (60-90 days)

3. **Add Probability Estimates:**
   - High (>60% chance)
   - Medium (30-60% chance)
   - Low (<30% chance)

### Priority 2 - Strategic Enhancements

1. **Add Competition Analysis:**
   - Who else is pursuing these grants?
   - What's our competitive advantage?
   - How to differentiate?

2. **Include Risk Assessment:**
   - What if we don't get funded?
   - Dependencies between grants
   - Backup options

3. **Enhance ROI Analysis:**
   - Hours to complete application
   - Probability of success
   - Expected value calculation

### Priority 3 - Usability Improvements

1. **Create One-Page Executive Summary** for each report
2. **Add Visual Timeline** showing all deadlines
3. **Include Success Story Templates** for applications
4. **Provide Email Templates** for initial funder contact
