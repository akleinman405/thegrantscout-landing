This application tracker helps Retirement Housing Foundation manage multiple capital funding applications simultaneously by organizing opportunities into four stages based on urgency and relationship status. The tracker was created from the 17 validated opportunities in your November 2025 Grant Opportunity Report, with special emphasis on the urgent November 25, 2025 Clark County Community Housing Fund deadline.
