## Section 3: Dual Positioning Strategy

### The Challenge
Arborbrook Christian Academy must navigate between two distinct funding worlds:
1. **Corporate/Secular Funders** - Prioritize community impact, access, inclusion
2. **Faith-Based Funders** - Value Christian mission, faith formation, discipleship

### Strategy: Two Application Personas

#### Corporate Funder Approach (DICK'S, Walmart, Panthers, FFTC)

**Lead With:**
- Youth development outcomes
- Community access and impact
- Number of students served
- Participation growth metrics
- Physical health and wellness benefits
- Character development through sports
- Leadership and teamwork skills

**Language to Use:**
- "Youth development"
- "Community access"
- "Removing barriers to participation"
- "Inclusive programming"
- "Character and leadership development"
- "Healthy lifestyles"
- "Academic support through athletics"

**Language to Avoid:**
- Heavy religious messaging
- Evangelism or conversion language
- Denominational terminology
- Scripture references
- "Christian formation" (use "character formation")

**Sample Narrative:**
> "Arborbrook Academy serves over [X] students in Matthews, NC through comprehensive K-12 athletics programming. Our sports programs remove barriers to participation, develop character and leadership skills, and create pathways for youth to lead healthy, active lives. In 2024-25, our athletics department served [X] student-athletes across [X] sports, providing access to organized team sports regardless of family financial circumstances."

---

#### Faith-Based Funder Approach (Neeb, Lorenz, Christian Foundations)

**Lead With:**
- Christian mission and identity
- Faith formation through athletics
- Discipleship and spiritual growth
- Biblical values in action
- Integration of faith and learning
- Character development rooted in Scripture
- Service and stewardship

**Language to Use:**
- "Christian education"
- "Faith formation"
- "Christ-centered athletics"
- "Biblical character development"
- "Discipleship through sports"
- "Whole-person education"
- "Glorifying God through athletics"

**Frame Athletics As:**
- Ministry opportunity
- Character formation vehicle
- Community witness
- Discipleship pathway

**Sample Narrative:**
> "At Arborbrook Christian Academy, athletics is an integral part of our Christ-centered educational mission. Our sports programs provide daily opportunities for faith formation, teaching student-athletes to glorify God through their physical gifts, develop Biblical character through competition, and serve others with humility and grace. We believe athletics uniquely develops perseverance, teamwork, and integrity - virtues rooted in Scripture and essential to Christian discipleship."
