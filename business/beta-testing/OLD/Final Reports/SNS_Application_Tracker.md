## About This Tracker

This application tracker helps Senior Network Services manage multiple grant applications simultaneously by organizing opportunities into four stages: Active Applications, Preparing (next 60 days), Planned (60-180 days), and Relationship Building (long-term). The tracker was created from the 15 validated opportunities in your November 2025 Grant Opportunity Report, with special emphasis on the December 12, 2025 California Senior Nutrition and Services Grant Program deadline and the January 14, 2025 San Mateo County AAA bidder's conference. Review this tracker weekly and update status as applications progress through each stage.
