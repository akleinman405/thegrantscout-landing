## About This Tracker

This application tracker helps Arborbrook Christian Academy manage multiple athletic equipment and program funding applications simultaneously. The tracker was created from the 26 validated opportunities in your November 2025 Grant Opportunity Report, organized by urgency and strategic value. Given the high volume of smaller grant opportunities, special emphasis is placed on efficient batch processing and maintaining both faith-based and corporate/secular positioning documents. Review this tracker weekly.

## Dual Positioning Documentation

### Positioning Document Management

Arborbrook must maintain TWO sets of positioning documents to maximize funding success across both faith-based and corporate/secular funders.

---

### Faith-Based Funder Version

**Use for:** Neeb Family Foundation, Lorenz Family Foundation, Herzog Foundation, other Christian foundations

**Documents Checklist:**

- [ ] **Mission Statement (Faith Version)**
  - Emphasizes Christian values and identity
  - References Christ-centered education
  - Includes faith formation language

- [ ] **Chapel/Spiritual Life Program Description**
  - Frequency and format of chapel services
  - Spiritual formation activities
  - Integration of faith in school life

- [ ] **Faith Integration in Athletics Philosophy**
  - How athletics serves Christian mission
  - Character development rooted in Scripture
  - Discipleship through sports ministry
  - Glorifying God through physical gifts

- [ ] **Denominational Affiliation Statement**
  - Statement of faith
  - Trinitarian commitment (required for Lorenz)
  - Church/denomination partnerships

- [ ] **Biblical Foundation Narrative**
  - Scripture-based approach to athletics
  - Character virtues emphasized
  - Service and stewardship focus

**Sample Narrative (Faith-Based):**
> "At Arborbrook Christian Academy, athletics is an integral part of our Christ-centered educational mission. Our sports programs provide daily opportunities for faith formation, teaching student-athletes to glorify God through their physical gifts, develop Biblical character through competition, and serve others with humility and grace. We believe athletics uniquely develops perseverance, teamwork, and integrity - virtues rooted in Scripture and essential to Christian discipleship."

---

### Corporate/Secular Funder Version

**Use for:** DICK'S Sports Matter, Walmart, FFTC Longleaf, Carolina Panthers, Nike, Duke Energy, NC Youth Sports Grant

**Documents Checklist:**

- [ ] **Mission Statement (Secular Version)**
  - Emphasizes academic excellence and character
  - Focuses on whole-child development
  - Highlights community contribution

- [ ] **Community Benefit Focus**
  - Students and families served
  - Geographic reach
  - Economic impact
  - Community partnerships

- [ ] **Diversity and Inclusion Language**
  - Access for all students
  - Removing barriers to participation
  - Inclusive programming approach

- [ ] **Youth Development Outcomes**
  - Leadership skills developed
  - Teamwork and collaboration
  - Health and wellness benefits
  - Academic support through athletics

- [ ] **Removed Explicit Faith References**
  - Generic "character development" vs. "Christian character"
  - "Values-based education" vs. "faith formation"
  - "Service" vs. "ministry"

**Sample Narrative (Corporate/Secular):**
> "Arborbrook Academy serves over [X] students in Matthews, NC through comprehensive K-12 athletics programming. Our sports programs remove barriers to participation, develop character and leadership skills, and create pathways for youth to lead healthy, active lives. In 2024-25, our athletics department served [X] student-athletes across [X] sports, providing access to organized team sports regardless of family financial circumstances."
