## SCORING CRITERIA

For each opportunity, evaluate and score based on:

1. **Mission Alignment**
   - Does this directly match the organization's core work?
   - Does it address their stated special needs?

2. **Eligibility Fit**
   - Geography match
   - Organization type match
   - Grant size appropriate for their capacity
   - Budget size alignment

3. **Deadline Feasibility**
   - Is there sufficient time to apply?
   - Does it fit their timeline needs?

4. **Competition Level**
   - How many likely applicants?
   - Do they have a realistic chance?

5. **Source Quality**
   - Is this a legitimate, established funder?
   - Clear application process?
   - Track record of funding?

6. **Funder giving liklihood**
   - based on giving patterns from the f990 data - does this funder 
      - Give to this type of nonproft?
      - Give to nonprofits they've never given to before
      - Is the application by invitation only or can the nonprofit apply?
