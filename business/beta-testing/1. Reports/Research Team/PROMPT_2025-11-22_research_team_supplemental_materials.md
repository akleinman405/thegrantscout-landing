```markdown
### Top 10 Opportunities At-a-Glance

| # | Funder | Amount | Deadline | Fit Score | Key Strength | Status |
|---|--------|--------|----------|-----------|--------------|--------|
| 1 | [Name] | $X-$Y | [Date] | XX/100 | [One compelling reason] | URGENT/HIGH/MEDIUM |
| 2 | [Name] | $X-$Y | [Date] | XX/100 | [One compelling reason] | URGENT/HIGH/MEDIUM |
...
```

**Column Definitions:**
- **#**: Priority ranking (1-10)
- **Funder**: Organization name (keep concise)
- **Amount**: Funding range
- **Deadline**: Specific date or "Rolling" or "TBD Q1 2026"
- **Fit Score**: Use the fit scores already in each deep dive (e.g., "95/100")
- **Key Strength**: ONE compelling reason in 3-8 words
  - Examples: "Recent $21.9M award to similar org", "Native Hawaiian youth precedent", "Incumbent provider advantage", "501(c)(3) now eligible", "Faith-based mission alignment"
- **Status**: 
  - URGENT = Deadline within 30 days
  - HIGH = Deadline 30-90 days OR rolling with high strategic value
  - MEDIUM = Deadline 90+ days OR lower strategic priority
