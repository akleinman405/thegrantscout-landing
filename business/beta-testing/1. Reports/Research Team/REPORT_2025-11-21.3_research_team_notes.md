# Research Team Notes - November 21, 2025

## Pre-Task Review (Agent 1 & Agent 2)

### Database Analysis Summary
- **TheGrantScout.opportunities**: 16,286 records
- **f990_data.pf_grants**: 21,011 grant records from 1,699 unique foundations
- **f990_data.f990_pf**: 2,229 unique private foundations

### Data Quality Observations

1. **Opportunities Table**:
   - Most opportunities (14,350) have NULL state - limited geographic filtering capability
   - California has most state-specific opportunities (1,880)
   - ~8,147 opportunities have past deadlines
   - ~4,587 opportunities have deadlines Nov-Dec 2025
   - ~968 opportunities have deadlines in 2026+
   - Many have Rolling or no deadline status

2. **Challenges Identified**:
   - North Carolina (Arborbrook) has very few opportunities in database
   - Hawaii (Ka Ulukoa) has only 21 opportunities
   - Need heavy web research for under-represented states
   - focus_areas field is sparsely populated
   - Limited eligibility criteria data

### Methodology Decisions

Given database limitations, we will use a **Hybrid Approach**:

1. **Database Query First** - Filter existing 16K+ opportunities for:
   - Geographic matches (state, national scope)
   - Mission-aligned keywords in title/description
   - Appropriate grant size ranges
   - Active or rolling status

2. **F990 Foundation Analysis** - Query pf_grants for:
   - Foundations that fund similar organizations
   - Typical grant sizes by foundation
   - Geographic giving patterns

3. **Web Research Supplement** - For organizations with:
   - Under-represented states (NC, HI)
   - Specialized needs (facility acquisition, athletics)
   - Large grant requirements ($500K+)
