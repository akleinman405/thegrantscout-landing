## RECOMMENDED BUSINESS MODEL: HYBRID RESEARCH PLATFORM

### Target Customer
Mid-market nonprofits ($500K-$3M annual budget) who find:
- Instrumentl too expensive ($12K+/year)
- GrantWatch too basic (outdated, limited features)
- OpenGrants unproven (new, limited track record)

### Pricing
**$100-150/month per client**
- 50-70% less than Instrumentl
- 3-5x more than GrantWatch
- Justified by research quality + automation speed

### Model
**70% Automated + 30% Manual Research**
- Algorithm generates Tier 1-2 matches (direct funders, peer-based)
- Research team validates Top 50, adds funder intelligence
- Quarterly custom research for active clients

### Economics (12 months)
- Target: 150-200 clients
- ARR: $180K-$360K
- Team: 5-7 FTE (2-3 researchers + 2-3 engineers + 1 PM)
- Break-even: Month 12-18

## CRITICAL RISKS & MITIGATIONS

### Risk 1: Automation Degrades Quality (60% probability)
**Mitigation:** Hybrid model with human validation, quality thresholds, continuous learning

### Risk 2: Team Scaling Dilutes Quality (50% probability)
**Mitigation:** Standardized training, peer review, vertical specialization, quality audits

### Risk 3: Scraping Fails/Blocked (40% probability)
**Mitigation:** Multiple sources, manual fallback, API partnerships, respectful scraping

### Risk 4: Competitor Response (40% probability)
**Mitigation:** Speed to market (3 months), vertical moats, partnership locks, differentiated messaging

### Risk 5: Pricing Mismatch (30% probability)
**Mitigation:** Tiered pricing, willingness-to-pay testing, ROI positioning
