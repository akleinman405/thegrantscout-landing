The National Institute for Occupational Safety and Health (NIOSH) R13 program provides targeted funding for scientific conferences addressing occupational health and safety. PSMF's World Patient Safety Summit is an ideal fit when positioned around healthcare worker safety - a topic that directly connects to NIOSH's mission and PSMF's expertise in systems-based safety approaches.

**Deadline: December 12, 2025 - 20 DAYS REMAINING**

|-----------|---------|
| **Funder** | National Institute for Occupational Safety and Health (NIOSH) |
| **Program** | Support for Conferences and Scientific Meetings |

| **Duration** | Single conference/meeting |
| **Deadline** | December 12, 2025 (5:00 PM local time) |
