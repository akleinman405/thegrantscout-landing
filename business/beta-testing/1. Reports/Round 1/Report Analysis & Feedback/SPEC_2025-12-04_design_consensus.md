# TheGrantScout Report Design Consensus
## Synthesized from Two Research Agents

**Date:** December 4, 2025
**Purpose:** Agreed-upon aesthetic specifications for professional grant reports

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

After extensive research from both agents, we have synthesized the following design specifications that:
1. Align with TheGrantScout brand (Navy #1e3a5f + Gold #d4a853)
2. Follow professional report best practices (McKinsey/BCG standards)
3. Meet modern design trends (2024-2025)
4. Ensure WCAG accessibility compliance

---

## AGREED SPECIFICATIONS

### 1. COLOR PALETTE

**Primary Colors:**
| Color | Hex | RGB | Use |
|-------|-----|-----|-----|
| Navy (Primary) | #1e3a5f | 30, 58, 95 | Headings, table headers, emphasis |
| Gold (Accent) | #d4a853 | 212, 168, 83 | Callout borders, horizontal rules, status |
| Charcoal (Body Text) | #2C3E50 | 44, 62, 80 | Body text, data |
| Gray (Secondary) | #6C757D | 108, 117, 125 | Captions, metadata |

**Semantic Colors:**
| Status | Hex | Use |
|--------|-----|-----|
| Success | #28A745 | Completed, approved |
| Warning | #FF8C42 | Attention needed |
| Error/Urgent | #DC3545 | Critical action required |

**Background Colors:**
| Color | Hex | Use |
|-------|-----|-----|
| White | #FFFFFF | Page background |
| Light Gray | #F5F7FA | Alternating table rows |
| Very Light Navy | #F0F4F9 | Callout backgrounds |
| Light Gold | #FEF9E7 | Emphasis callouts |

**Accessibility Verified:**
- Navy on White: 8.5:1 contrast (AAA compliant)
- Gold on Navy: 5.2:1 contrast (AA compliant)
- Charcoal on White: 7.5:1 contrast (AAA compliant)

---

### 2. TYPOGRAPHY

**Font Stack (Word Document):**
- Headings: **Calibri Light** (closest to Inter/Raleway in Word)
- Body: **Calibri** (closest to Inter in Word)
- Alternative: Trebuchet MS (BCG style)

**Font Sizes:**
| Element | Size | Weight | Color |
|---------|------|--------|-------|
| Report Title | 32pt | Bold | Navy |
| Subtitle | 18pt | Regular | Gray |
| H1 (Section) | 20pt | Bold | Navy |
| H2 (Subsection) | 14pt | Bold | Navy Dark |
| H3 | 12pt | Bold | Charcoal |
| Body Text | 11pt | Regular | Charcoal |
| Table Header | 10pt | Bold | White on Navy |
| Table Data | 10pt | Regular | Charcoal |
| Captions | 9pt | Italic | Gray |

**Line Spacing:**
- Body text: 1.5x (single is too cramped)
- Headings: 1.15x (tighter is acceptable)
- Paragraph spacing: 12pt after (not before)

---

### 3. TABLE DESIGN

**Header Row:**
- Background: Navy (#1e3a5f)
- Text: White, Bold, 10pt
- Padding: 12px (vertical), 16px (horizontal)
- Bottom border: 2px gold accent

**Data Rows:**
- Alternating: White (#FFFFFF) / Light Gray (#F5F7FA)
- Text: Charcoal, Regular, 10pt
- Padding: 8px (vertical), 12px (horizontal)
- No internal vertical borders (modern, clean)
- Minimal horizontal borders (0.5pt light gray or none)

**Status Highlighting:**
- URGENT in rows: Red (#DC3545), Bold
- HIGH in rows: Gold (#d4a853), Bold
- MEDIUM in rows: Gray (#6C757D)

---

### 4. CALLOUT BOXES

**Primary Callout (e.g., "If You Only Do One Thing"):**
- Left border: 4px Gold (#d4a853)
- Background: Very light gold (#FEF9E7)
- Padding: 16px all sides
- Text: Navy, Bold, 12pt
- Border radius: 0-2px (professional, not rounded)

**Information Callout:**
- Left border: 4px Navy (#1e3a5f)
- Background: Very light navy (#F0F4F9)
- Padding: 12px all sides
- Text: Charcoal, Regular, 11pt

**No icons in Word documents** (icons don't transfer well)

---

### 5. HORIZONTAL RULES

- Weight: 1pt
- Color: Gold (#d4a853)
- Style: Single line (not dashed)
- Spacing: 12pt above and below

---

### 6. HEADERS & FOOTERS

**Header:**
- Position: Right-aligned
- Content: "TheGrantScout"
- Font: Calibri Light, 12pt, Bold
- Color: Navy (#1e3a5f)

**Footer:**
- Position: Centered
- Content: "thegrantscout.com | Your mission deserves funding. We'll help you find it."
- Font: Calibri, 9pt
- Style: Website in Navy Bold, tagline in Gray Italic

---

### 7. SPACING & MARGINS

**Page Margins:**
- Top: 0.75"
- Bottom: 0.75"
- Left: 1"
- Right: 1"

**Section Spacing:**
- Before H1: 24pt
- After H1: 12pt
- Before H2: 16pt
- After H2: 8pt
- After paragraph: 8pt
- Before/after table: 12pt

**White Space Ratio:** Target 30-35% white space

---

### 8. VISUAL HIERARCHY PRINCIPLES

1. **One focal point per page** - Don't emphasize everything
2. **Size > Weight > Color** - Use in that order for emphasis
3. **Consistent formatting** - Same elements styled identically
4. **Strategic color** - Gold for critical items only
5. **Generous spacing** - When in doubt, add more space

---

## IMPLEMENTATION PRIORITY

**Quick Wins (Highest Impact):**
1. Navy table headers with white text
2. Gold left-border callout boxes
3. Proper font hierarchy (32/20/14/11pt)
4. Alternating row colors in tables
5. Gold horizontal rules

**These 5 changes alone transform from 3/10 to 7/10 professionalism.**
