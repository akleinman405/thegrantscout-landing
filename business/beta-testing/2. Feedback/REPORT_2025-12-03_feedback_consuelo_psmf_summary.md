# TheGrantScout Feedback Call Summary
**Date:** December 3, 2025
**Participant:** Consuelo (PSMF - Patient Safety Movement Foundation)
**Duration:** ~22 minutes

---

## Key Takeaways

### 1. Organization Profile Challenges
- PSMF has a **broad, multi-faceted mission** that makes grant matching difficult
- They operate like "several nonprofits wrapped into one" with diverse programs:
  - Fellowship program (training healthcare professionals worldwide)
  - Healthcare access initiatives
  - Patient safety advocacy
  - Research activities
- **Geographic scope is worldwide**, adding complexity to grant eligibility

### 2. Current Grant-Seeking Challenges
- Many grants target **specific populations** (e.g., seniors, specific regions) that don't align with PSMF's broader approach
- Example: Grants focused on "seniors in California" are too narrow when PSMF serves all vulnerable populations globally
- Healthcare access grants often expect traditional clinic models, which doesn't match PSMF's approach
- It's difficult to quantify impact for complex programs vs. simple metrics like "meals delivered"

### 3. Historical Grant Success
- Past success has come primarily through **relationship-based funding** (board connections, established networks)
- Now seeking to **diversify funding sources** beyond personal networks
- Want to move toward application-based grants rather than relying solely on relationships

### 4. Preferred Grant Approach
- **Foundation-focused approach preferred** over opportunity-focused
- Interested in "quick intro proposal" strategy to multiple foundations
- Prefers letter of inquiry (LOI) format over lengthy applications
- Strategy: Send brief pitches to many foundations, see who responds, then invest time in promising leads

### 5. Most Promising Match Identified
- **Art Foundation** was highlighted as the best match from the initial report
- Focus: Older adults in California, health and social services access
- Appeals to PSMF because:
  - Serves vulnerable populations in healthcare
  - Allows LOI submission format
  - Program aligns with PSMF's mission

### 6. Priority Program for Funding
- **Fellowship Program** identified as key focus area
- Grown from 4 fellows in first year to 18 currently
- Free to participants (no fees charged)
- Significant staff time investment but low direct costs
- Vision: Build global network to improve healthcare systems

## Product Insights for TheGrantScout

### What's Working
- The matching concept is valuable
- Foundation information is helpful for relationship building

### Areas for Improvement
- Need better handling of **multi-mission organizations**
- Consider **foundation-first** matching (relationship building) vs. opportunity-first (application-based)
- LOI/inquiry format opportunities may be more valuable than formal applications for exploratory-stage nonprofits

### User Need Identified
- Organizations in "discovery phase" need:
  - Low-investment outreach options (LOIs, inquiries)
  - Foundation relationship mapping
  - Contact/networking information
  - Less focus on specific deadlines, more on foundation fit
