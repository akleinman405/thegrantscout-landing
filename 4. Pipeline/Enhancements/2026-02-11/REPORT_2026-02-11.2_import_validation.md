## Duplicate Analysis and Deduplication

### Root Cause

ZIP 04A was partially imported (~23%), then the process was killed and restarted from the beginning of 04A. ZIP-level checkpointing means the interrupted ZIP is re-processed entirely, creating duplicates for the portion already imported.

### Pre-Dedup State

| Table | Total 2024 Records | Unique EIN+TaxYear | Duplicates | Duplicate % |
|-------|--------------------|--------------------|------------|-------------|
| pf_returns | 137,203 | 126,366 | 10,837 | 7.9% |
| nonprofit_returns | 611,107 | 574,037 | 37,070 | 6.1% |

94% of PF duplicates were concentrated in ZIP 04A (10,238 of 10,837).

### Deduplication Executed

Strategy: For each EIN+tax_year pair with duplicates, keep the row with the highest `id` (most recent insert, imported with fixed parser code). All foreign keys use `ON DELETE CASCADE`, so child rows are automatically cleaned up.

## Notes

- The 62.3% population rate for governance indicators (conflict of interest, whistleblower) on nonprofit_returns is correct: these fields exist only on Form 990, not 990-EZ. The 62.3% exactly matches the 990/(990+990-EZ) ratio.
