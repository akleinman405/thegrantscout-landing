- **Education/training** model (not direct service)
- **Toolkit and fellowship** approach

**Insight:** Sibling grants revealed what foundations fund for regional patient safety orgs, but PSMF competes in a different tier (national foundations, fellowship funders).

## Methodology Lessons Learned

### What Worked

| Approach | Result |
|----------|--------|
| **Reading Consuelo feedback first** | Immediately knew fellowship was priority, geographic restrictions were dealbreakers |
| **Searching for fellowship-specific grants** | Found Moore Foundation (9/10) and Gillings (7/10) that sibling analysis missed |
| **Using specific keywords** ("fellowship", "healthcare education", "preventable") | Surfaced grants sibling approach missed |
| **Understanding what PSMF is NOT** | Created clear disqualification criteria |

### What Didn't Work As Well

| Approach | Issue | Better Alternative |
|----------|-------|-------------------|
| **Sibling-only analysis** | Most siblings are regional orgs funded by regional funders | Supplement with keyword search for fellowship/education grants |
| **Broad "patient safety" search** | Too many irrelevant results (medication safety, equipment, etc.) | Use specific terms: "fellowship", "training healthcare professionals" |
| **Grant amount alone** | High-dollar grants often for direct service, not training | Filter for education/fellowship purpose first |

### Recommended Grant Matching Process

```
Step 1: Understand Client Deeply
├── Read feedback calls (what they WANT)
├── Build organization profile (what they DO)
├── Define target grant purpose (what they NEED)
└── Identify what they're NOT (disqualifiers)

Step 2: Search Strategy
├── Sibling analysis (finds funders in their space)
├── Keyword search (finds program-specific funders)
│   └── Use client's priority program keywords
└── Foundation research (finds mission-aligned funders)

Step 3: Evaluate Each Grant
├── Does scope match? (geographic, topical)
├── Does model match? (education vs. service)
├── Does purpose match? (specific program alignment)
└── Would client have qualified? (size, approach, sector)

Step 4: Rank and Recommend
├── Score 8-10: Actively pursue
├── Score 5-7: Consider with positioning adjustments
└── Score 1-4: Do not pursue
```
