## Methodology

### 1. Sibling Identification
- Started with embedding-based search (91 candidates)
- Removed false positives (labor unions, athletic clubs)
- Manually verified 40 true siblings in 3 tiers:
  - Tier 1: Core Patient Safety Organizations (20)
  - Tier 2: Healthcare Simulation & Education (10)
  - Tier 3: Healthcare Quality Collaboratives (10)

### 2. Sibling Grant Analysis
- Loaded 40 siblings into `calc_client_siblings`
- Pulled grants from `fact_grants` (35 grants from 12 siblings)
- Analyzed grant purposes against PSMF needs

### 3. Broader Grant Search
- Searched `fact_grants` for keywords: "fellowship", "patient safety", "healthcare education", "quality improvement", "preventable"
- Filtered for $50K-$500K grants from 2020+
- Identified foundations funding similar programs to PSMF's fellowship

### 4. Matching Criteria
- Healthcare education/training focus
- National/global scope (not geographically narrow)
- LOI-friendly giving culture
- Appropriate grant size ($50K-$500K)
- Track record funding similar organizations
