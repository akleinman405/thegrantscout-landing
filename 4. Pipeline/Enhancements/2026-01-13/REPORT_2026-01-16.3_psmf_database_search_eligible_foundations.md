## Key Finding: The Invite-Only Problem

### The Reality of Healthcare Fellowship Funding

The database search identified many foundations that fund fellowship programs and healthcare quality work. However, web verification revealed that **most major healthcare fellowship funders operate on an invite-only basis**:

| Type | Count | Examples |
|------|-------|----------|
| Invite-only | 6 | Hartford, Doris Duke, Schooner, JPB, Blue Shield CA, Medtronic |
| Paused/No current opps | 2 | Doctors Company, RWJF |
| Open to applications | 3 | Macy, Brinson, Greenwall |

### Why This Matters for PSMF

1. **The best-fit foundations (Hartford, Doctors Company) are not currently accessible**
2. **Open foundations have US-only restrictions** that conflict with PSMF's global fellowship
3. **Relationship-building is critical** - many funders require warm introductions

### Strategic Implications

PSMF has two paths forward:

**Path A: Work Within Constraints**
- Apply to Macy/Brinson with US-focused framing
- Wait for Doctors Company to reopen
- Build relationships with invite-only funders

**Path B: Find Different Funders**
- Search for foundations that explicitly fund GLOBAL health education
- Look at international funders (Wellcome Trust, etc.)
- Explore corporate foundations with global mandates
