## Notes

### Why These 15?

1. **Mission alignment** - All fund global health, healthcare quality, or medical education
2. **Clear introduction paths** - Each has an identifiable connection point
3. **Mix of accessibility** - From direct email (Schooner) to relationship-building (Rockefeller)
4. **California concentration** - Several CA funders for CA-based PSMF
5. **Medical device peers** - Joe Kiani's industry network is an asset

### What's NOT on This List

- **Gates Foundation** - Too large/competitive, no clear intro path
- **Ford Foundation** - <1% unsolicited success rate
- **MacArthur Foundation** - Invite-only, no clear connection
- **Duke Endowment** - PSMF not eligible (hospitals only, NC/SC only)
- **RWJF** - No current opportunities, shifted priorities

### Total Potential

If PSMF builds relationships with all 15 foundations over 2-3 years:
- **Combined annual giving:** ~$1.6B
- **Realistic PSMF target:** $500K - $5M+ across multiple funders
- **Highest ceiling:** Skoll Award ($1.5M), Rockefeller grant ($5M+)
