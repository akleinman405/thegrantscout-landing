## Notes

### Why Relationship-Building vs. Open Applications?

1. **Higher dollar potential** - Skoll ($1.5M awards), Rockefeller ($10M+ grants)
2. **Better mission alignment** - These funders want global health impact
3. **Long-term partnerships** - Multi-year funding vs. one-time grants
4. **No US-only restrictions** - Can fund Kiani Fellowship as-is

### The 6-12 Month Investment

Relationship-building takes time, but the payoff is significant:
- **Skoll Award:** $1.5M unrestricted
- **Rockefeller grant:** $1-5M for global health systems
- **Hartford/Moore:** $500K-2M for healthcare quality
