## Notes

### Key Strategic Insights

1. **Match on PROGRAM TYPE, not CAUSE AREA** - Search for fellowship funders, not patient safety funders

2. **PSMF's global scope is both a challenge and an asset** - Limits US foundation access but opens global funders

3. **The 4,793 committed hospitals are underutilized** - Hospital foundations, partnership grants, in-kind support

4. **Joe Kiani's network is a major asset** - Medical device industry, foundation boards, patient safety advocates

5. **Relationship building is essential** - The best-fit global funders require warm introductions

### What We Learned for Future Reports

1. **Eligibility + Open Applications = First Filter** - Don't waste time on invite-only or ineligible foundations

2. **Database tells WHO funded, not WHO will fund** - Web verification essential for current accessibility

3. **"Structurally unique" orgs need creative matching** - Sibling-based matching may fail; match on program type instead

4. **Global orgs need US-framing strategy** - Most accessible US foundations require US activities
