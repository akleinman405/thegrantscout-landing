**What Each Report Contains:**

**Executive Summary**
- "If You Only Have 5 Hours" - your single highest-ROI action
- Priority ranking of all 5 foundations

**5 Foundation Profiles**, each with:
- Why this foundation fits your work (with specific grant evidence)
- Funder snapshot: annual giving, typical grant size, repeat funding rate, giving trend
- How to apply: LOI process, portal links, or relationship-building path
- Key contacts: program officers and grants managers with emails
- Positioning strategy: how to frame your request for best results
- Action steps: numbered tasks with time estimates

**Monthly Action Plan**
- Week-by-week breakdown of tasks
- What you'll be waiting on after submissions
- Dependencies mapped out

**Quick Reference**
- All contacts in one table
- All deadlines summarized
- Time estimates per foundation
