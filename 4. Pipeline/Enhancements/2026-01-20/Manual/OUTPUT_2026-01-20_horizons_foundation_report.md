## Appendix: Methodology

### How We Found These Foundations

**Step 1: Identified "Hyperlocal" Funders**
- Foundations with 80%+ of grants staying in-state
- Located in states where Horizons has affiliates

**Step 2: Filtered for Education/Youth Focus**
- Grant purposes containing: youth, education, summer, enrichment, K-8, after-school
- Recipients in NTEE sectors B (Education), O (Youth Development), P (Human Services)

**Step 3: Verified Accessibility**
- Confirmed open application processes via foundation websites
- Excluded invitation-only foundations

**Step 4: Ranked by Fit**
- Weighted scoring: Track record (35%), Geography (25%), Program fit (25%), Eligibility (15%)
- Selected top 5 with strongest evidence of fit

### Data Sources

- IRS Form 990-PF filings (2020-2024)
- Foundation websites
- LinkedIn (contact verification)
- TheGrantScout database (8.3M grant records)

### Limitations

- 2021 data unavailable for Yawkey and Gilbert Foundations
- El Pomar 2022-2024 data appears aggregated (not individual grants)
- Contact emails are inferred from patterns; verify before sending
