## Executive Summary

Identified 5 foundations with veteran-related grant history for Matt to explore beyond the existing VetsBoats report. All 5 have email addresses confirmed directly on their websites or across multiple grant directories. Total assets across the 5 foundations: $3.0B.

Key finding during research: VetsBoats (EIN 464194065) is a **private non-operating foundation** (990-PF filer), not a public charity. This means granting foundations must exercise expenditure responsibility when funding VetsBoats. Analysis of VetsBoats' 5 known funders confirmed they all successfully granted to VetsBoats despite this status, with grants ranging from $2,500 to $25,000.

## Methodology

1. **SQL query** on `f990_2025.fact_grants` for foundations with grants matching veteran/therapeutic/sailing/maritime keywords (tax_year >= 2020, assets > $1M, open to applications)
2. **Excluded** 5 report foundations + 5 known funders + VetsBoats itself
3. **Pulled contact info** from `pf_returns` (app_contact_email, officers, phone, website)
4. **Web verification** of every email via foundation websites and grant directories
5. **Iterated** through 3 rounds of replacements to achieve 5/5 high-confidence emails
