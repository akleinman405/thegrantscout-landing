## 3. The Heinz Endowments

**Location:** Pittsburgh, PA
**Assets:** $2.1B
**Contact:** Grants Team
**Email:** grants@heinz.org (confirmed on heinz.org/grants)
**Phone:** (412) 281-5777
**Website:** [heinz.org](http://www.heinz.org)

**Why:** One of the largest foundations in the country with a dedicated veterans portfolio. Funds veteran programs at $200K-$300K levels, including Veterans Leadership Program of Western PA, Veterans Research Foundation, and RAND Corporation veterans policy work. Their veteran focus is policy/services-oriented rather than therapeutic recreation, but the scale and veteran commitment make them worth a conversation.
