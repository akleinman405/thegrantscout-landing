## Data Quality Notes

1. **All Bay Area:** Revenue range $250K-$5M with NTEE B%/O5/P3 produced 300+ Bay Area orgs, so LA/SD/Sac weren't needed. Could generate a separate export for those metros.
2. **Officer title prioritization:** Executive Director > CEO > President > Founder > Director > Chief > VP. Excluded: Treasurer, Secretary, Board Member, Trustee.
3. **Email bodies partial:** Only 12 of 24 emailed orgs have email bodies in the generated_emails CSVs. The other 12 were from the earlier Nov 2025 campaign which used a different system (no body stored).
4. **No replies or bounces** in this specific 300-org set (1 bounce was in the broader CA youth/ed segment but didn't make the Bay Area top 300).
