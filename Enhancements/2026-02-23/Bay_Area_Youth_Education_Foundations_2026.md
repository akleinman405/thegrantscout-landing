## About This List

This list identifies the top 150 private foundations in the Bay Area that have funded youth and education programs between 2022 and 2024. Together, these foundations awarded $1.5 billion across 6,881 grants to organizations working in K-12 education, scholarships, literacy, after-school programs, and youth development.

All figures reflect giving specifically to youth and education programs. Grant data comes from IRS 990-PF filings for tax years 2022 through 2024, the most recent available.
