## Findings

### F1 (Important) -- schema.md: Wrong PK for cohort_foundation_lists_v2

**Location:** `.claude/rules/schema.md` line 131
**Issue:** Listed `PK: cohort_id` but `cohort_foundation_lists_v2` has no `cohort_id` column (that was the v1 table). The table also has no PK or unique constraint.
**Verification:** `pg_constraint` query returned zero rows; `information_schema.columns` confirmed no `cohort_id` column.
**Fix:** Changed to `No PK. Logical key: (state, ntee_sector, foundation_rank).`
