## Notes

1. **"Open + grants to orgs" is very low (47):** The `grants_to_orgs` flag may be under-populated in dim_foundations. The 1,149 "open to applications" count is the more useful number for list building.
2. **np2 count (33,781) > nonprofit_returns count (31,085):** np2 may include orgs from additional sources or different deduplication logic.
3. **City name normalization needed:** Foundation city names have mixed case. Any public-facing list should normalize these.
4. **Grant keyword matching is broad:** Terms like "education" and "school" cast a wide net. A curated freebie list should manually verify the top foundations for genuine youth/education focus.
