## Context / Situation

Government funding to nonprofits has been massively disrupted in 2025-2026 through executive orders, agency freezes, and budget proposals. This creates both a crisis and an opportunity for TheGrantScout:

- Nonprofits losing government funding need private foundation alternatives
- We can identify the most vulnerable orgs using our database
- We can match them with foundations active in their sectors

## Tasks

### Phase 1 (This Prompt)
Build a comprehensive knowledge base documenting:
1. What government funding was cut (timeline, EOs, sector-by-sector breakdown)
2. Database methodology for identifying impacted nonprofits
3. Foundation landscape analysis (capacity by sector)
4. Phase 2 roadmap for org-level identification and matching

### Phase 2 (Future)
Use the knowledge base to identify specific orgs, quantify impact, and generate foundation matches.
