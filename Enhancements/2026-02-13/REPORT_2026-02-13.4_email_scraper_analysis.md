## Executive Summary

Conducted deep analysis of the email scraping pipeline (scripts 04-07) with rigorous web research and three independent agent reviews (business value, technical risk, data quality). Answered all 7 questions with data-backed recommendations.

**Top findings:**
- Scraper accuracy is ~85-90% deliverable, but the real gap is **coverage** (only 13.4% of foundations have websites)
- MX validation does NOT update confidence score (confirmed gap)
- Commercial email verification API ($100-750) is better than self-hosted Reacher (saves 4-6 hrs, no IP risk)
- Database tables don't exist yet (CRITICAL -- pipeline has only been smoke-tested on 10 orgs)
- Only 16GB free disk space vs 14GB needed for full HTML cache
- Officer data is 77% ALL CAPS with truncated titles -- cross-reference harder than expected (12-16 hrs)
- Foundation and nonprofit email ranking should be different (grants@ for foundations, person@ for nonprofits)

**Roadmap reprioritized:** Foundation contact intelligence first (product value), campaign improvements second.
