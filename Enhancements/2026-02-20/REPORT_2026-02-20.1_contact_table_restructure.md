## Executive Summary

Added two new contact table structures to the grant report format:

1. **Contact Quick Reference** (new section under Time Allocation Guide): One primary contact per foundation with Foundation, Contact, Title, Email, Phone, Website columns. Provides at-a-glance lookup for the client.

2. **All Contacts** (restructured from old format): Expanded from 5 rows (one per foundation) to 13 rows with multiple contacts per foundation. New columns: Foundation, Name, Role, Email, Phone, Website. Replaces the old "Email/Phone" combined column.

Also fixed a CSS issue in `md_to_pdf.py` where wide 6-column tables would overflow the page by adding `overflow-wrap: break-word` and `word-break: break-word` to table cells.
