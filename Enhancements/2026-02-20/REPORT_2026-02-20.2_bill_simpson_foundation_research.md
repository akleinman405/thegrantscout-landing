## Executive Summary

The **Bill Simpson Foundation** (EIN 843992087) is an active private foundation in Brownsburg, IN with $18.4M in assets, making ~$600K/year in rolling grants across diverse causes. **SignatureFD** is an Atlanta-based wealth management firm ($9.6B AUM) that was hired in 2023-2024 as the foundation's philanthropic advisor, replacing Foundation for the Carolinas as administrator. **Elizabeth Burdette, JD, CAP** is SignatureFD's Philanthropic Advisor who leads their SignatureGENEROSITY practice and now serves as the Bill Simpson Foundation's designated application contact. She is the best first point of contact for grant inquiries.
