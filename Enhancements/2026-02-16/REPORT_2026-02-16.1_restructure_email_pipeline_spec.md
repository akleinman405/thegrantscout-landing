## Executive Summary

Consolidated 6 source documents (4 session reports from Feb 9-13, email system documentation, email scraper analysis) into a single organized technical reference: `SPEC_email_pipeline.md`. Then restructured from 5 phases to 6 phases to better reflect the actual workflow from raw data to closed deals.

The spec now covers 6 pipeline phases: (1) URL discovery, (2) email/contact extraction and validation, (3) prospect profiling, (4) campaign content, (5) email delivery, (6) response tracking. It documents 7+ Python scripts, database tables across 2 schemas, known issues, and an improvement roadmap prioritized by business value.

- **Status flags** per phase (DONE / BUILT / PARTIAL / NOT STARTED) for quick scanning
- **Database quick-reference** consolidates all tables from both schemas in one place, now with Phase column
- **Improvement roadmap** pulled from scraper analysis, organized into 4 phases (A-D) by business value, cross-referenced to pipeline phases
