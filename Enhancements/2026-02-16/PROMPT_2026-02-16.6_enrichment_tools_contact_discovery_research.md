# PROMPT 2026-02-16.6 - Enrichment Tools & Phone/Address-Based Contact Discovery for Foundations

**Date:** 2026-02-16
**Type:** Research
**Context:** Verbal instruction via Claude Code CLI

## Task

Research enrichment tools and phone/address-based contact discovery methods for foundations. We have ~95K private foundations with NO website URL but DO have phone numbers (85,600), mailing addresses (95,494), officer names from 990-PF filings, and EINs. Need to find email addresses and identify the right contact person for grant inquiries. Many are small family foundations ($100K-$5M assets).

## Topics to Research

1. Bulk Email/Phone Enrichment APIs (Hunter.io, ZoomInfo, Apollo.io, Clearbit/Breeze, Pipl, FullContact)
2. USPS Address Intelligence (residential/commercial classification, SmartyStreets, Lob)
3. LinkedIn as Officer Lookup Tool (Sales Navigator, PhantomBuster, scraping legality)
4. People Search / Whitepages APIs (Whitepages Pro, BeenVerified, Spokeo)
5. Nonprofit-Specific Data Cooperatives (GrantStation, Instrumentl, Candid)
6. Physical Mail Effectiveness (response rates, cost per piece, EDDM)
7. Reverse Phone Lookup (Trestle, Twilio Lookup, NumVerify)

## Expected Output

Structured research summary with ratings (1-5) for each approach on: coverage of small family foundations, cost per lookup, data quality, legal safety, and implementation effort.
