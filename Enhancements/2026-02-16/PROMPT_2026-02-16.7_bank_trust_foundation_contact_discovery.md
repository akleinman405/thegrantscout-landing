# PROMPT_2026-02-16.7 - Bank-Managed & Trust Foundation Contact Discovery Research

**Date:** 2026-02-16
**Type:** Research
**Prompt Number:** 7

## Context

About 13,242 of our ~95K no-website foundations (13.9%) are bank/trust-managed. We need to understand how grantseekers currently reach these foundations and whether there are scalable contact discovery paths.

## Tasks

Research these topics:
1. How grantseekers currently contact bank-managed foundations
2. Major bank trust foundation programs (JPMorgan, BofA, Wells Fargo, BNY Mellon, US Bank, Northern Trust, PNC, Truist)
3. Foundation management companies (fsrequests.com, FoundationSource, etc.)
4. Donor-advised fund sponsors
5. Attorney/CPA as contact path
6. Community foundation affiliations

For each approach, assess scalability, cost, likelihood of reaching decision-maker, and whether it's standard practice.
