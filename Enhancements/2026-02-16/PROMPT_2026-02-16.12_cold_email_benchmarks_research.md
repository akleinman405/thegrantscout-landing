# PROMPT: Cold Email Benchmarks & Best Practices Research

**Date:** 2026-02-16
**Prompt Number:** 12
**Type:** Research

## Context

TheGrantScout is building an AI-generated cold email pipeline to reach nonprofit prospects and foundation contacts. Anchor KPI is positive reply rate. Need quantified benchmark data and evidence-based best practices to inform email strategy.

## Tasks

1. Research cold email benchmark data (2025-2026) from major platforms
2. Subject line strategies with data
3. Email length and structure optimization
4. Cadence and follow-up timing
5. Personalization levels with quantified impact
6. Common mistakes that kill cold emails
7. Nonprofit/foundation-specific cold email insights

## Expected Output

3-4 page strategy brief with benchmark summary table, subject line recommendations, email structure template, cadence blueprint, personalization framework, kill list, and sector-specific insights. All findings cited with source URLs.
