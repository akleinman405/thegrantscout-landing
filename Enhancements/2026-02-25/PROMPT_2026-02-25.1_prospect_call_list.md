# PROMPT: Generate 100-Nonprofit Cold Call Prospect List (National)

**Date:** 2026-02-25
**Sequence:** .1

## Context

Generate a CSV of 100 nonprofits to cold call as potential TGS clients ($99/mo grant matching service). Target orgs are 501(c)(3) public charities related to veterans, sailing/maritime, aviation, and recreational/adaptive therapy. National scope with geographic diversity (cap ~15 per state).

## Requirements

1. All states, 501(c)(3) public charities only (exclude private foundations)
2. Keywords: veteran, sailing, sailboat, maritime, aviation, airplane, flight, recreational therapy, adaptive sport/recreation, equine therapy, adventure therapy, wounded warrior
3. Only include orgs with GO viability (adjusted foundation_count >= 50 after 0.85 haircut)
4. Include best officer contact (ED > CEO > President > Development Director > Director priority)
5. Require non-null phone and clean website (exclude N/A, NONE, 0)
6. Cap ~15 orgs per state for geographic spread
7. Sort by existing funder count descending (proven grant seekers first)

## Output

CSV with columns: Org, Priority (blank), Date Contacted (blank), Phone (XXX-XXX-XXXX), Contact, Title, Email (blank), State, Timezone (mapped from state), Dependents (blank), EIN, Rating (blank), Website, About (1-2 sentences)
