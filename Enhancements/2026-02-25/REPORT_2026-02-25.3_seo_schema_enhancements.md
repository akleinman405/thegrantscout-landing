1. **FAQPage schema completed:** Added 4 missing FAQ items so the schema now has all 12 FAQs matching the visible page content

## Changes Made

### 1. FAQPage Schema: 8 items -> 12 items

Added these 4 FAQ entries that existed in `page.tsx` but were missing from the JSON-LD schema:

| # | Question | Position |
|---|----------|----------|
| 2 | "What information do I need to provide?" | After "How does TheGrantScout find relevant foundations?" |
| 6 | "Do you guarantee I'll get grants?" | After "Can I cancel my subscription anytime?" |
| 7 | "How current is your foundation data?" | After "Do you guarantee I'll get grants?" |
| 8 | "How do I get started?" | After "How current is your foundation data?" |

The FAQ order in the schema now matches the order displayed on the page exactly.

### 2. Service Schema Enhancements

Added two new properties to the `serviceSchema` object:

**`brand`** (Schema.org Brand type):
- `name`: "TheGrantScout"
- `logo`: "https://thegrantscout.com/favicon.svg"

**`serviceOutput`** (Schema.org Report type):
- `name`: "Monthly Grant Matching Report"
- `description`: "Curated foundation opportunities with funder profiles, giving history, contact information, and positioning strategies."
