## Methodology

### Data Source
`nonprofits_prospects2` (np2) table joined to `nonprofit_returns` for latest revenue. 673K pre-flattened nonprofit records with officer contacts, mission descriptions, and program descriptions.

### Keyword Search
Searched `mission_description`, `program_1_desc`, `program_2_desc`, and `program_3_desc` for:

| Category | Keywords |
|----------|----------|
| Veterans | veteran, wounded warrior |
| Sailing/Maritime | sailing, sailboat, maritime |
| Aviation | aviation, airplane, flight |
| Adaptive Therapy | equine therapy, adventure therapy, adaptive sport, adaptive recreation, recreational therapy |

### Filters Applied

1. **Public charity only:** EIN not in `pf_returns` (excludes private foundations)
2. **Revenue cap:** Latest `nonprofit_returns.total_revenue <= $5M` (ED handles fundraising at this size)
3. **Phone required:** Non-null phone number
4. **Website required:** Non-null, excludes N/A, NONE, 0, empty
5. **Contact required:** At least one of ED/CEO, President, or Chair name present
6. **GO viability:** `cohort_viability.foundation_count * 0.85 >= 50`
7. **State cap:** Max 15 per state via `ROW_NUMBER() OVER (PARTITION BY state)`
8. **Sorted by:** Existing funder count descending (proven grant seekers first)

### Contact Priority
Used np2's pre-extracted officer fields: ED/CEO > President > Chair (name and title). At under $5M revenue, these leaders typically handle or directly oversee fundraising.

## Notes

- **$5M cap rationale:** At orgs under $5M, the ED/CEO typically handles or directly oversees fundraising. Above $5M, you'd need to reach a Development Director or Grants Manager, which IRS data doesn't reliably capture.
- **Better category mix** than v2: The revenue cap removed large national orgs (Wounded Warrior Project, etc.) that dominated the veterans category, allowing more sailing/maritime (14%) and aviation (11%) orgs to surface.
- **$2M-$5M is the sweet spot** (62% of list): Large enough to afford $99/mo, small enough that the ED values grant prospecting help.
- **30 additional viable orgs** are available in the query buffer (130 found total) if the list needs expansion.
