## Executive Summary

Identified 25 private foundations that actively provide capacity building grants to nonprofits with limited funding sources (1-2 funders only). These foundations represent strong B2B partnership opportunities for TheGrantScout, as they fund organizations that may benefit from grant prospecting services.

**Key Metrics:**
- Foundations identified: 25
- Total capacity building grants analyzed: 913,743 (11% of all grants in database)
- Geographic spread: 15 states represented
- Combined assets: $60B+

---

## Methodology

1. **Identified capacity building grants** using keyword matching on purpose_text:
   - capacity, organizational development, general operating, operating support
   - infrastructure, strategic planning, leadership development
   - management assistance, technical assistance, capacity building

2. **Calculated recipient funder counts** to find nonprofits funded by only 1-2 different foundations

3. **Filtered foundations** that gave capacity building grants to at least 10 recipients with limited funding sources (2020-2024)

4. **Researched each foundation's website** to find:
   - Team/staff page URL
   - Phone number
   - Best contact person for grant inquiries

## Notes

### Data Limitations

- Web research was conducted using automated fetch; some foundation websites blocked access or had different URL structures
- Some contact names come from 990 filings rather than current website data
- "Recipients with limited funders" counts recipients who received capacity building grants from this foundation AND are only funded by 1-2 total foundations in our database

### Recommendations

1. **Priority outreach**: Start with regional/community foundations (Otto Bremer, El Pomar, Bangor Savings, Sewall) which are more accessible than mega-foundations

2. **Personal contacts available**: McKnight (Neeraj Mehta), Moore (Ignacio Estrada), Walton (Sara Guillaume), Pohlad (Brian Paulson) have specific grants contacts identified

3. **Email contacts confirmed**: W.K. Kellogg (conciergedesk@wkkf.org), McKnight (apply@mcknight.org), Kresge (grantsmanagement@kresge.org), Sewall (grants@sewallfoundation.org)

### Excluded from List

Large corporate foundations (Bank of America, JPMorgan Chase, Cigna) were excluded despite high recipient counts as they typically have less personalized grantmaking relationships.
