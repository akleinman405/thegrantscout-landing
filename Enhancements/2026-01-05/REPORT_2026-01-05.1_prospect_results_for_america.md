## Executive Summary

**Rating: POOR - Skip this prospect**

Results for America is a **large, well-funded, and growing nonprofit** with $32M annual revenue, $41M in assets, and a sophisticated development team. They are NOT struggling from the loss of Edna McConnell Clark Foundation funding. In fact, they have **thrived** since 2021, more than doubling their revenue.

The Edna McConnell Clark Foundation gave them $4M total (2019-2020), representing only ~10% of their foundation funding in those years. They have replaced this funding many times over with major gifts from Gates Foundation ($23M), Bloomberg ($12M), and other mega-funders.

This organization has a VP of Development, Director of Development, and Associate of Development - a full development team. They do not need TheGrantScout's services.
