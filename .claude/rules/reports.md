# Report Rules - TheGrantScout

Guidelines for generating client-facing grant matching reports.

---

## What Clients Value

- **Discovery:** Finding NEW foundations (not ones they already know)
- **Fit:** Clear explanation of WHY this foundation matches their work
- **Actionability:** Contact info, deadlines, recent similar grants
- **Organization:** Info in one place, even if they knew some opportunities

---

## What Makes a Bad Report

- Wrong grant names (e.g., CSNSGP = Security, not Nutrition/Services)
- Expired deadlines included
- Broken links
- Foundations client already has relationships with
- Foundations that don't fund their geography
- Foundations inactive for 3+ years

---

## Required Elements (Every Report)

- [ ] Each match includes: foundation name, EIN, assets, location
- [ ] Each match includes: 2-3 recent similar grants with amounts
- [ ] Each match includes: contact info or application URL
- [ ] No foundations from client's existing relationships
- [ ] All foundations have given grants in past 3 years
- [ ] All deadlines are current (not expired)
- [ ] All links verified working

---

## Quality Checks Before Delivery

- [ ] Spell-check completed
- [ ] Dollar amounts formatted consistently ($X,XXX)
- [ ] No duplicate foundations within report
- [ ] Grant examples are relevant to client's focus areas
- [ ] Grant names are accurate (verify against source)

---

## Report Types

### Opportunity-Focused
- Active grants with deadlines
- Best for: Orgs ready to apply now
- Content: Deadline, requirements, positioning strategy

### Foundation-Focused
- Foundation profiles for relationship building
- Best for: Orgs preferring LOI approach
- Content: ~5 foundations with deep intelligence, connection paths

---

## Funder Snapshot Metrics

Each foundation profile should include:

| Metric | Source |
|--------|--------|
| Annual Giving | fact_grants aggregation |
| Typical Grant | Median, min, max from history |
| Geographic Focus | % in-state, top states |
| Repeat Funding Rate | % recipients funded 2+ times |
| Giving Style | % general support vs. program-specific |
| Recipient Profile | Typical budget size, sector focus |
| Funding Trend | Growing/stable/declining over 3 years |
| Comparable Grant | Similar org that received funding |

---

## Three Customer Types

| Type | Characteristics | What They Want |
|------|-----------------|----------------|
| Calendar Managers | Experienced grant writers, know most opps | Organization, reminders, nothing overlooked |
| Niche Seekers | Looking outside usual sources | Discovery of new/unexpected funders |
| Relationship Builders | Prefer prospecting over formal apps | Foundation profiles, LOI strategy, networking |
