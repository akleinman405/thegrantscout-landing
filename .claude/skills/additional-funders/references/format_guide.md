# Additional Funders - Format Guide

Use this format for the additional funders companion document.

---

## Document Structure

```markdown
# [Organization Name]
# Additional Foundation Leads

**Companion to:** Monthly Grant Opportunities Report ([Month Year])
**Foundations Listed:** [N]
**Data Source:** IRS Form 990-PF filings (2016-2024)

---

## How to Use This Document

These foundations scored well in our matching algorithm but didn't make the top 5
for this month's report. They represent your broader pipeline for future outreach.

**Next steps for any foundation that interests you:**
1. Visit their website (linked below)
2. Review their recent grants for fit
3. Let us know which ones to profile in depth next month
```

## Column Definitions

| Column | Content | Example |
|--------|---------|---------|
| Foundation | Name with website link | [Smith Foundation](https://example.org) |
| State | 2-letter state code | CA |
| Annual Giving | Average annual grant-making | $2.3M |
| Median Grant | Median individual grant size | $25,000 |
| Why Relevant | 1 line: sector match, geography, comparable grantee | "Funded 8 youth athletics orgs in HI since 2020" |

---

## Tier Assignment Rules

| Tier | Criteria |
|------|----------|
| **A: Strong Fit** | Model score in top quartile AND (state match >= 20% OR sector match >= 30%) |
| **B: Good Fit** | Model score in second quartile OR strong on one dimension only |
| **C: Worth Watching** | Model score in third quartile but has a notable characteristic |

Foundations in the bottom quartile should not be included.

---

## "Why Relevant" Examples

Good:
- "Funded 12 senior housing orgs in CA, median $40K"
- "Growing funder: +45% giving over 3 years, expanding to your sector"
- "Funded [Similar Org Name] $75K for operating support (2023)"

Bad:
- "Makes grants" (too vague)
- "Large foundation" (not relevant to client)
- "Might be interested" (hedging)
